# Joe Dispenza

Dr Joe Dispenza is a researcher, lecturer, and chiropractor who works the intersection of neuroscience, epigenetics, and the observation that mind and body together can heal what medicine alone cannot. The biographical pivot he tells most often: a biking accident with a serious back injury, the medical recommendation for surgery to fuse vertebrae, and his decision to apply the cosmology he had been studying (consciousness creates form) to his own body for three months instead. He recovered without surgery. The recovery itself is not the teaching; what he made of it is — that the same practice anyone can run can engage the same intelligence that put a spine back together.

From that seed, his four books traced an arc: *Evolve Your Brain* (2007) on neuroplasticity; *Breaking the Habit of Being Yourself* (2012) on the self-image as a behavioural loop; *You Are the Placebo* (2014) on the documented healing power of belief; and *Becoming Supernatural* (2017) on the specific meditation technology — coherent body-mind-emotion states — that lets a body experience states beyond ordinary perception. The seven-day advanced retreats are where the practice is taught at depth; the research conducted there has produced over **20,000 brain scans** and **10,000+ heart-rate-variability measurements** — an unusual archive for a non-clinical context.
