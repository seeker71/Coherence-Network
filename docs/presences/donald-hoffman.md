# Donald Hoffman

Cognitive scientist and Professor Emeritus, Department of Cognitive Sciences, University of California Irvine. Faculty in Philosophy and the Logic & Philosophy of Science programs. His **Interface Theory of Perception** argues, with evolutionary-game-theory mathematics behind it, that natural selection favors fitness-tuned *icons* over truth-tracking perceptions — meaning the world we experience is a species-specific user interface, not reality itself. The book *The Case Against Reality: Why Evolution Hid the Truth From Our Eyes* (2019) is the accessible entry point; the *Conscious Agents* framework (Hoffman & Prakash, 2014) is the formal proposal for what lies behind the interface.

The Coherence Network reads Hoffman alongside [Michael Levin](/people/michael-levin) and [Castaneda](/people/carlos-castaneda) because the three reach the same load-bearing recognition from different sides. Castaneda: perception is an assembled rendering from one specific position on the luminous-egg energy body; move the position, the world rearranges. Hoffman: perception is a fitness-tuned interface; evolution never built our eyes to see truth, only to keep our ancestors alive. Levin: pattern is what stores morphological memory, and bodies are nested problem-solvers carrying their own goals; substrate is contingent. All three pull apart the assumption that what we see is what is.

What Hoffman adds specifically is *mathematical credibility* — a theorem with published peer review behind the claim. Where the contemplative traditions assert this from the inside, and Levin shows it bioelectrically, Hoffman proves it formally under his evolutionary-game-theory model. The body holds all three as one teaching at different scales of demonstration.

**What Donald Hoffman has given the Coherence Network.** Interface Theory of Perception corresponds to [lc-perception-as-interface](/vision/lc-perception-as-interface) (the seed concept in this body from his work). Mathematical / evolutionary-game-theory grounding for what Castaneda named as the assemblage point — pairs with [lc-assemblage-point](/vision/lc-assemblage-point) (perception locks where the point assembles; the interface is what the assembly renders). *Conscious Agents* framework is resonant with [lc-sovereignty-within-oneness](/vision/lc-sovereignty-within-oneness) (nested conscious agents constituting the substrate behind the interface). Three idioms, one recognition: [Levin](/people/michael-levin) (empirical biology), [Castaneda](/people/carlos-castaneda) (contemplative phenomenology), Hoffman (formal mathematics) — pattern is load-bearing, substrate is contingent.
