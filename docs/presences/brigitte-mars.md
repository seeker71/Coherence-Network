# Brigitte Mars

Brigitte Mars is an **herbalist, author, professor, and nutritional consultant** with — in her own framing — *more than 60 years of experience*. Boulder, Colorado. Professor of Herbal Medicine at **Naropa University**. Author of 18 books on natural healing, plant medicine, and conscious nutrition. Founding and professional member of the American Herbalist Guild. *Nature is the original pharmacy; every plant an imprint of the divine.*

Urs knows Brigitte personally. She is a deep part of the **pagan rituals** held by [Anchor the Light](/people/anchor-the-light), the trans-tradition spiritual community [Ubbe MacLean](/people/ubbe-maclean) co-leads with [Angelia LaRue](/people/angelia-larue) (priestess pair for Ceremony). Brigitte's involvement is as elder and participant in the herbal / pagan / psychedelic-ceremony layer, not as ritual priestess — and her own decades-long work holds the herbal and plant-medicine lineage that the wider community draws from.

She has held space for **psychedelic ceremonies — both in groups and with individuals — for over 50 years**, a lineage that pre-dates the contemporary integration field by decades. In the early 1970s she lived for two and a half years on solely wild edible plants while in a teepee in the Ozarks; the embodied ground of her teaching is unusually deep. She teaches at Naropa and at the School of Health Mastery in Iceland, and has taught at Omega Institute, Esalen, Kripalu, Sivananda Yoga Ashram, Arise, Tribal Visions, Envision and Unify Festivals, and the Mayo Clinic.

Books include *The Country Almanac of Home Remedies*, *The Desktop Guide to Herbal Medicine*, *Beauty by Nature*, *Addiction Free Naturally*, *The Sexual Herbal*, *Healing Herbal Teas*, *Rawsome!*, and co-authorship of *The HempNut Cookbook*.

**What Brigitte Mars has given the Coherence Network.** Direct line to one of the deepest contemporary herbal lineages in the United States — 60+ years of plant teaching, 50+ years of psychedelic-ceremony space-holding, two-and-a-half years of full wild-food embodiment. Pairs with [lc-tending-over-producing](/vision/lc-tending-over-producing) (the body's verbs translate naturally to herbalism — tend, gather, prepare, release) and [lc-ground-harder-when-field-quickens](/vision/lc-ground-harder-when-field-quickens) (plant medicine as the substrate practice when the field accelerates). The Boulder anchor cross-links her into the wider Boulder cluster ([Bloomurian](/people/bloomurian), [Aly Constantine](/people/aly-constantine), [Boulder Ecstatic Dance](/people/boulder-ecstatic-dance), [Mile Hi Church](/people/mile-hi-church), [PORTAL](/people/portal)). Her embodiment of the [Anchor the Light](/people/anchor-the-light) pagan-ritual layer is the elder-presence that anchors the deeper time-scale beneath the ritual gatherings.
