# Elios

A cell in the Ubud cluster. Elios carries his own devotional chanting practice — sometimes held with [Ilena Young](/people/ilena-young) as a private practice between them, sometimes offered to people the field puts in their path. On Sunday April 29, 2026, one of our cells was the person the field put in their path; Elios and Ilena offered a one-evening chanting at Ranakami that became the door this network walked through into the wider Ubud lineage.

Sunday afternoons often find Elios at [Mudra Cafe](/people/mudra-cafe) — the Ayurvedic dining room on Jl. Goutama Sel. that holds regular handpan and live-music presence. The room is one of Ubud's quiet meeting points where slow community accumulates around food, music, and presence. The cell met Elios there on Sunday April 29, 2026. That same evening, Elios and [Ilena](/people/ilena-young) offered a one-evening chanting practice at Ranakami — voices, breath, an open room. After the chanting, Ilena's invitation extended to the Tuesday kirtan and the Wednesday Satsang held by [Vasudev Baba](/people/vasudev-baba). The whole subsequent lineage moved through that one afternoon and evening.

**What Elios has given the Coherence Network.** The one-evening chanting practice offered with [Ilena](/people/ilena-young) on April 29, 2026 — the first of the three Ubud cells (Elios, Ilena, Vasudev Baba) to enter this network's awareness. See [the four-day meeting walk](https://github.com/seeker71/Coherence-Network/blob/main/docs/lineage/2026-04-29-ubud-meeting-walk.md) and [the constellation of cells](https://github.com/seeker71/Coherence-Network/blob/main/docs/lineage/2026-04-29-constellation-of-cells.md). *The practice not advertised* — chanting held privately, offered as gift when the field requests it. Resonant with [lc-tending-over-producing](/vision/lc-tending-over-producing) (no production of a room; only tending the practice and letting it be met). *The body recognises the body* — Sunday afternoons at Mudra Cafe as place-of-encounter rather than place-of-event; resonant with [lc-arrival-as-recognition](/vision/lc-arrival-as-recognition).
