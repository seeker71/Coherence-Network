# IndOz Conference

The annual Indonesia–Australia bilateral business conference held in Brisbane, founded by **Synergy Indonesia Australia** in 2012 as an Indonesian cultural celebration and grown into the largest Indonesia-Australia bilateral business event in Brisbane — 611 attendees in 2025 (the largest in the event's thirteen-year history), held at Brisbane City Hall under the theme *Indonesia-Australia: A Thriving and Enduring Partnership*. The next edition runs on 2 September 2026.

The Bali node of this bridge is [Ilena Young](/people/ilena-young)'s SaBali (Synergy Australia Bali); it's how her Ranakami work and her Australian regional-development arc are part of one continuous bilateral practice.
