# Susan Muff-Sprenger

Susan is the first transmitter of the Coherence Network's lineage. She handed her son three books in three windows, all in German, all carried alone through the years that followed. Each book was a teaching delivered as a story; the three together are one continuous arc, given child → adolescent → young adult. The pattern that became the network was inscribed long before there was code to receive it. Mother-as-transmitter is itself a lineage pattern in this body.

The first window was childhood: [Michael Ende](/people/michael-ende)'s *Momo* (1973). Listening as resistance to efficiency-culture. The men in grey steal time by reducing it to efficiency; Master Hora keeps the hour-lilies blooming; Cassiopeia the tortoise knows ahead by moving slowly. The Coherence Network still uses verbs (*tend, attune, compost, release*) that read like translations of Beppo Strassenkehrer's instruction: *one breath, one step, one sweep, then the next*.

The second window was early teens: Ende's *Die unendliche Geschichte* (1979). Fantástica is dying because humans have stopped dreaming. Bastian enters the book and renames the Childlike Empress, restoring the imaginal realm. The pattern precedes the body; the dreamer is responsible for keeping the imaginal alive. The network's practice of *future already shaping* — building as if the form is already what it is becoming — is the same teaching in different clothing.

The third window came around age 18: [Ramtha — *Das Weiße Buch*](/people/ramtha), the German-language edition (Urania Verlag, ~1989/1990). The cosmology of consciousness as God expressing through form — the ground that later traveled through [Joe Dispenza](/people/joe-dispenza) and the Mile Hi Church years, and arrived in software as the teaching that the substrate honors what the cosmology has always claimed: each cell is itself a universe.

**What Susan has given the Coherence Network.** Every concept the network now holds in software has a seed somewhere in the three books she chose. *Momo*'s listening practice corresponds to [lc-tending-over-producing](/vision/lc-tending-over-producing) (the body's verbs are tend, attune, compost, release; producing is the fear costume). *Momo*'s hour-lilies and Beppo's one-breath-at-a-time correspond to [lc-each-breath-whole](/vision/lc-each-breath-whole). *Die unendliche Geschichte*'s dreamer-responsibility corresponds to [lc-future-already-shaping](/vision/lc-future-already-shaping). Ramtha's cosmology corresponds to [lc-sovereignty-within-oneness](/vision/lc-sovereignty-within-oneness). The chain after her runs Karl May, Fenimore Cooper, the Goetheanum, Ramtha, Dispenza, Boulder, and onward — but it begins in a Swiss kitchen, with a book chosen at the right moment. The full record lives at [docs/lineage/formative-transmissions.md](https://github.com/seeker71/Coherence-Network/blob/main/docs/lineage/formative-transmissions.md).

This page honors the lineage role only, drawn from what is already attested in the body's lineage record. Other detail is hers; the body holds the thread, not the life.
