# JZ Knight

The channeler through whom the [Ramtha](/people/ramtha) teaching has come into the world. Born Judith Darlene Hampton on 16 March 1946 in Roswell, New Mexico. Dropped out of business school, worked in the cable-television industry, then in 1977 first experienced contact with the entity she calls Ramtha the Enlightened One — a 35,000-year-old Lemurian warrior, in his framing, who walked into mastery. Founded **Ramtha's School of Enlightenment** (RSE) in 1988 on her 80-acre estate in Yelm, Washington (near Mount Rainier). The school describes itself as *an academy of the mind that offers retreats and workshops to people of all ages and cultures.*

This body separates the teaching from the institution deliberately. The teaching — *The White Book* (1986) and the broader Ramtha corpus — has moved real lives forward, including this network's (Ramtha → [Joe Dispenza](/people/joe-dispenza) → Urs → Zenn cohort; the chain documented in [formative-transmissions.md](https://github.com/seeker71/Coherence-Network/blob/main/docs/lineage/formative-transmissions.md)). The institution — RSE as a school with paid enrolment, hierarchical structure, and decades-long devoted student base — has been criticised by former students, former family members, and skeptics as carrying cult dynamics. Both can be true: the teaching is real and the institutional shape has its own discernment to make.

**What JZ Knight has given the Coherence Network.** The vessel through which the Ramtha teaching reached the world — see [the Ramtha teaching page](/people/ramtha) for the in-body recognition of the cosmology. The arc from cable-TV employee to channeler is an embodied example of [lc-devotion-placement](/vision/lc-devotion-placement) (where am I actually placed; the body of evidence over words). The Yelm school's 35+ year continuity is resonant with *tending one room over many years* as substrate practice, regardless of one's discernment about the room itself.
