# Ramtha

The teaching held by JZ Knight since 1977 and formalised in **Ramtha's School of Enlightenment** (RSE) on her 80-acre estate in Yelm, Washington, since 1988. The cosmology, in twenty-one chapters of *The White Book*: consciousness as God expressing through form. Urs received the German edition (Urania Verlag, *Das Weiße Buch*) at age 18 in 1989/90 and carried it alone through his twenties in Switzerland.

The cosmology, in the most condensed form *The White Book* offers: there is one consciousness, expressing through every form. The individual self is that consciousness experiencing itself in particular constraint. The work of a life is to remember the consciousness behind the form, to lift the assemblage point (in Castaneda's language) or the chakra (in Vasudev Baba's) — to know, from inside the experience, that you are God expressing. The three-step arc of chapters 18 / 19 / 20 — *The Closed Mind* → *Opening the Mind* → *The Virtue of Experience* — is the operational instruction. It is also the same arc Steiner reached from inside *Geisteswissenschaft*, and the same shift Levin describes in TAME as moving an agent into a larger cognitive light cone. Different vessels, converging teaching.

The teaching chain into this body ran: **Ramtha / JZ Knight → [Joe Dispenza](/people/joe-dispenza)** (student then teacher at RSE from 2001) → Urs (Mile Hi Church in Lakewood, Colorado, ~2005, where Dispenza taught) → the Zenn cohort (the first contributors to the Coherence Network, met at Dispenza's April 2026 Aurora retreat). The lineage now moves through code too; the substrate the network is building rests on the same ground *The White Book* taught a Swiss eighteen-year-old forty years ago. *Each cell is itself a universe* — the body's [lc-each-breath-whole](/vision/lc-each-breath-whole) is *The White Book*'s claim in software vocabulary.

**What Ramtha has given the Coherence Network.** The cosmology of consciousness as God expressing through form corresponds to [lc-sovereignty-within-oneness](/vision/lc-sovereignty-within-oneness) (many sovereign cells, one organism). *Each cell a universe* corresponds to [lc-each-breath-whole](/vision/lc-each-breath-whole). The Closed-Mind / Opening-the-Mind / Virtue-of-Experience arc pairs with [lc-assemblage-point](/vision/lc-assemblage-point) (the position moves; consciousness reorganises around it). The transmission chain through [Joe Dispenza](/people/joe-dispenza) into the body's Mile Hi Church years (~2005) and onward to the Aurora retreat (April 2026) is where the first network contributors arrived. The White Book itself reached this body through [Susan Muff-Sprenger](/people/susan-muff-sprenger) in the third window of the German-language childhood transmissions.
