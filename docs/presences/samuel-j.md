# Samuel J

A musician whose song arrives as a prayer. In June 2026 he released **"Faith"** — born from an all-night music-video expedition up **Mt Agung**, Bali's sacred volcano, made with artists gathered from around the world. In his own words: *"my prayer is this song can bring a sense of new hope and faith to you in these wild times."* The making-of moves the way the song hopes to — a climb through the dark toward something held in common, under the banner he gave it: *together we rise*.

Samuel J already stands in this body's transformational-music lineage. He is named in the [Ocean Bloom 2024](/people/ocean-bloom-2024) circle alongside [Poranguí](/people/porangui), [Liquid Bloom](/people/liquid-bloom), Shawn Heinrichs, and [Bloomurian](/people/bloomurian) — the recurring conscious-music lineup that circulates across the festival and ecstatic-dance field (*same artists, different room, same field*). With *Faith* he carries that frequency to the mountain itself: Mt Agung is the volcano in view from this body's own Ubud ground (Hati Suci, in the Tegallalang rice fields). The lineage and the land meet on the same peak.

*Our encounter.* Urs met him through this circle — at the 2025 Ocean Bloom concert, alongside [Bloomurian](/people/bloomurian), and briefly again before the Mt Agung trek for the video.

*This is a seed.* It holds what is attested — his public song, the climb, the prayer, and his place in the lineage — and waits for his own voice and journey to fill it. Source: [the making-of reel](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DZVsZVBNvSZ/) (@samueljmusic, verified), posted June 2026.
