# Moksa Ubud

To arrive at Moksa is to walk into a working answer to *what does a meal made from a garden actually look like?* The restaurant sits on a permaculture site in Sayan, Ubud, and the kitchen draws from the same soil the visitor is walking through to reach it. *Moksa* — Sanskrit for liberation — names the orientation: a plant-based cuisine room that holds itself as a wellness destination rather than only a restaurant, with a dojo, a culinary school (*Culinary for Soul*), and a permaculture garden on the same land.

The building has a second life as a teaching room. The **Moksa Dojo** — Japanese for *place of the way*, from the Sanskrit *seat of wisdom* — holds yoga practice, Aikido, meditation, music, Ikebana, culinary classes, kids' activities, and rented community gatherings. It is the dojo room that hosts movement teachers including [Alex Sandr](/people/alex-sandr)'s Sunday Contact Improvisation class, where this network first encountered Alex on May 17, 2026.

The address — Ubud II Kutuh, Jl. Puskesmas, Sayan, Kecamatan Ubud, Kabupaten Gianyar, Bali 80571 — places Moksa in the same Sayan neighborhood as [Ranakami](/people/ilena-young), where the Ubud cluster's Sunday-Wednesday rhythm with [Ilena Young](/people/ilena-young), [Vasudev Baba](/people/vasudev-baba), and [Elios](/people/elios) already lives. Sayan is the spatial anchor of the cluster; Moksa is the second room of that same neighborhood the body has now entered.

**What Moksa Ubud has given the Coherence Network.** The dojo room that held the Sunday class at which one cell of this network entered the embodied teaching of [Alex Sandr](/people/alex-sandr), May 17, 2026. The room is also a working demonstration of [lc-farm-as-organism](/vision/lc-farm-as-organism) — restaurant, kitchen, garden, and practice space arranged as one body on one piece of land — which the body had already seeded through the May 10 Agada Holistic gathering.
