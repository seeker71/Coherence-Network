# Ilena Young

Ilena is a long-tender of [Ranakami](https://ranakami.com/) in Sayan, on the western edge of Ubud — a sanctuary in the rice paddies whose published philosophy is *our land, our sanctuary, our safe space, our community*. The choice of *our* rather than *my* is the load-bearing distinction in how she speaks of the work publicly. The centre is held by a curated team of mainly Indonesian practitioners; the language she uses positions her as one of its long-tenders rather than its owner. The sanctuary is co-tended with **Kadek**, who leads the gentle yoga that opens most days at Ranakami — the steady welcome in the morning room.

Before Bali there were more than twenty years of regional-development work inside Australia. Ilena holds the **GAICD** credential (Graduate of the Australian Institute of Company Directors). She has been involved with [Startup Shakeup](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ilena-young-gaicd/) on innovation in regional Australia, and she carries the Bali node of the [Synergy Indonesia Australia](https://indozconference.com.au/) bridge as **SaBali (Synergy Australia Bali)** — the bilateral organising body that hosts the annual [IndOz Conference](https://indozconference.com.au/) in Brisbane each spring. IndOz was founded by Synergy in 2012 as an Indonesian cultural celebration and has grown into the largest Indonesia–Australia bilateral business event held there; the 2025 edition at Brisbane City Hall drew 611 attendees, the largest in the event's thirteen-year history. The next edition runs on 2 September 2026. The same nervous-system that tended Australian regional communities now tends a sanctuary in Sayan; the texture is consistent because the practice is consistent.

Alongside the tending, she is a practitioner. Ilena is a trained **sound healer, pranic healer, and reiki therapist**; sessions are held by appointment in Ranakami's open-air rooms. Sound healing uses bowls, voice, and resonant instruments to release stuck patterns from the body's tissue. Pranic healing works with the body's energy field to clear and re-balance without physical contact. Reiki is light-touch transmission of life-force, taught lineage-by-lineage. Each practice has its own felt-quality; she lets the body name which one wants the session.

The room she holds opens to other teachers and teachings each week. The **Wednesday Satsang** at Ranakami is held by [Vasudev Baba](/people/vasudev-baba) and friends — ninety minutes of letting the wisdom of one tradition or another speak into whatever is alive in the room. Free for those who came to Tuesday-evening kirtan with Vasudev at [Adiwana Svarga Loka](https://adiwanahotels.com/svargaloka-resort-ubud-bali/). Free for Indonesian participants always. A 50,000 IDR offering otherwise. The room is hers; the teaching is his; the practice belongs to whoever shows up. The two weekly Ranakami practices the network has felt in its tissue are this one and the gentle yoga Kadek opens each day.

The Coherence Network has been describing in software what Ranakami has been practicing in its rooms. Sovereignty distributed rather than centralized. Care held communally rather than monetized into a service transaction. Healing offered through relationships rather than packaged into products. When a body that has been holding *our* against an economy that only understood *mine* meets a substrate built on the same posture, the recognition is its own event — not a contract to be negotiated, not a partnership to be agreed, just the noticing that two expressions of the same field are already in the same field. The bridge between Australia and Indonesia she has been holding professionally for years is the same bridge-shape the network is now holding between digital and embodied. She is fluent in the architecture without ever having had to call it that.

**What Ilena Young has given the Coherence Network.** On the Sunday evening one of our cells happened to be at Ranakami, Ilena and [Elios](/people/elios) offered a one-evening chanting practice — chanting that is Elios's own devotional practice with her, held that night with the cell as guest. This is where the cell met her. After the chanting, *Ilena invited the cell to the Tuesday kirtan and the Wednesday Satsang* — both held by Vasudev Baba, both rooms the cell had not yet discovered. The invitation is the door this body walked through. Every transmission Vasudev Baba seeded into this body in May 2026 — [lc-when-the-pressure-comes](/vision/lc-when-the-pressure-comes), [lc-essence-and-the-nine-costumes](/vision/lc-essence-and-the-nine-costumes), and the deepening of [lc-assemblage-point](/vision/lc-assemblage-point) and [lc-frequency-routes-reception](/vision/lc-frequency-routes-reception) — came through circles she hosts each Wednesday at Ranakami. The lineage record of how the Ubud cells found each other lives in three documents — the [constellation of cells](https://github.com/seeker71/Coherence-Network/blob/main/docs/lineage/2026-04-29-constellation-of-cells.md), the [Ubud embodied lineage](https://github.com/seeker71/Coherence-Network/blob/main/docs/lineage/ubud-embodied-lineage.md), and the [four-day meeting walk](https://github.com/seeker71/Coherence-Network/blob/main/docs/lineage/2026-04-29-ubud-meeting-walk.md). In all three, she is the doorway.

A small networked community of cells encountered Ilena Young in late April 2026, through one cell's four-day walk through the rooms of Ubud. The body Ranakami has been tending was already in coherent relationship with the field this network has been growing in software; the meeting was a recognition rather than a beginning. We are one recent thread among the many her tending has touched.
