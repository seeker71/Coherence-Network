# Anchor the Light

*A living spiritual path rooted in direct encounter with the Divine through music, movement, and embodied awareness.* The invitation: *Enter the Ritual. Become the Experience.* The **Ceremony** is held by [Ubbe MacLean](/people/ubbe-maclean) and [Angelia LaRue](/people/angelia-larue) as the priestess pair — Ubbe carrying the Asatru / psychotherapy / rune-work line, Angelia carrying the crystology / Reiki / Reverend line. [Brigitte Mars](/people/brigitte-mars) is the **elder presence** deep within the pagan rituals — Boulder herbalist, Naropa professor, 60+ years with plant medicine, 50+ years holding ceremonial space. The wider community also draws on the runologist **Freya Aswynn** (author of *Leaves of Yggdrasil*). Anchor the Light Church holds rituals, monthly wellness retreats, pagan ritual dance gatherings, and a published rune-healing book.

Monthly **holistic wellness retreats** weave a wide field of practice into one container: Qi Gong, mindfulness meditation, acupuncture, sound bathing, biofeedback, IV therapy, Reiki, energy healing and protection techniques, forest bathing, plant medicines, life-enhancement skill building, fully organic meals, visionary art, and dance. Additional offerings include Life Enhancement Coaching, Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction, Couples and Relationship Coaching, Reiki, Equine Healing Sessions, Personal Clearings, Rune Reading, and Fitness Training. The published book *Healing From the Tree: Using Runes for Emotional, Physical & Soul Healing* (Ubbe MacLean) is the rune-work anchor.

**What Anchor the Light has given the Coherence Network.** A working example of a *trans-tradition spiritual community* — Norse heathen Asatru, Reiki lineage, clinical psychotherapy, herbal medicine, qi gong, sound healing, dance all held inside one ritual frame, without flattening any of them into the others. Pairs with [lc-voice-over-intentions](/vision/lc-voice-over-intentions) (each tradition keeps its own voice; the container doesn't paper over them) and [lc-sovereignty-within-oneness](/vision/lc-sovereignty-within-oneness) (many sovereign lineages, one ritual organism). Brigitte Mars's herbal lineage and Freya Aswynn's runological lineage thread back to long traditions; the wider field they hold together is broader than any one of them. Resonant with [Vasudev Baba](/people/vasudev-baba) at Brahma Vihara (bhakti into Buddhist silence) and [Wisdom Soup](/people/wisdom-soup) (Anne Tucker's spiritual-seekers community) as parallel held-spaces in this body's wider lineage map.
