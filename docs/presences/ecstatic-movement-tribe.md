# Ecstatic Movement Tribe

*Where solo became relational*

The recurring room held by [Tammy Beattie](/people/tammy-beattie) at [Vali Soul Sanctuary](/people/vali-soul-sanctuary). The container in which this body&apos;s practice crossed from solo ceremonial floor — the kind of room first met through [Liquid Bloom](/people/liquid-bloom) and the first [Pagan Ritual](/people/pagan-ritual) — into relational dance: eye contact, weight-sharing, the beginning of moving *with* other bodies. EMT is the tribe whose practice opened the door to [Contact Improv](/people/contact-improv).
