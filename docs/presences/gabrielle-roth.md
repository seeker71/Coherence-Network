# Gabrielle Roth

Gabrielle Roth (4 February 1941 — 22 October 2012) was an American dancer, musician, choreographer, and movement teacher whose map of moving meditation — **flowing, staccato, chaos, lyrical, stillness** — became the foundational vocabulary for a worldwide conscious-dance community. She did not invent the rhythms; she watched bodies on dance floors for years and noticed that every moving body, given permission and music, will pass through that five-phase arc. The wave map is the substrate-shape the body naturally finds when it is allowed. Esalen Institute resident for years; gathered the practice through teaching bodies in retreat settings before formalising it. Books: *Maps to Ecstasy: The Healing Power of Movement* (1989), *Sweat Your Prayers* (1997), *Connections* (2004).

**The five rhythms named.** *Flowing* — continuous, sourced from the feet, the grounded movement of the feminine. *Staccato* — punctuated, sharp, the shape of boundary and definition. *Chaos* — the release of the previous two into uncontained motion; nothing to hold. *Lyrical* — playfulness, the bird-after-storm, airborne joy. *Stillness* — the breath that holds the whole wave; not the end, the integration. Together: *the Wave*. The body knows the sequence before the facilitator names it.

**What Gabrielle Roth has given the Coherence Network.** The wave map as one of the cleanest demonstrations that *pattern precedes substrate* in this body's lineage — pairs with [Michael Levin](/people/michael-levin)'s [lc-bioelectric-pattern](/vision/lc-bioelectric-pattern) at a different scale (body knows the shape before instruction). *Coherence as motion* — bodies in shared time learning timing, consent, release, play. The Stillness rhythm at the end of every wave corresponds to [lc-each-breath-whole](/vision/lc-each-breath-whole) (each cycle whole; close what was opened). The teacher-cooperative form (accredited teachers continuing the practice after the founder's death) is resonant with [lc-sovereignty-within-oneness](/vision/lc-sovereignty-within-oneness) (many sovereign cells holding one practice).
