## Choosing a Method

**For the hearth (kitchen)**: Cob (thermal mass holds heat, sculptural curves), timber frame (open spans), earth oven
**For nests (dwellings)**: Earthship (off-grid complete), cob (beautiful/personal), straw bale (fast/insulated)
**For the workshop**: Timber frame (open spans, movable walls), bamboo in tropics
**For gathering circles**: Stone foundation, open-air or minimal roof, earth floor
**For sanctuary**: Cob (acoustics, curves), rammed earth (massive silence)
