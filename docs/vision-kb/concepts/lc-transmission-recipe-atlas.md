# Transmission Recipe Atlas -- Portable State-Change Patterns

> Every source can be read as a recipe for changing state. The atlas
> keeps the reading honest by naming the observer, the extraction, the
> transposition, and the proof mode in the same breath.

## Summary

[`lc-grammar-is-the-universal-recipe`](lc-grammar-is-the-universal-recipe.md)
names the substrate doorway: every structured carrier can parse into a
recipe tree. [`lc-observable-resonance-flow`](lc-observable-resonance-flow.md)
names how an arrival leaves yield after observation. This concept names
the human-facing practice that sits between them: a visible atlas of
recipes extracted from songs, stories, videos, outages, healing
modalities, embodiment practices, specs, and teachings.

The atlas does not flatten those domains into one proof standard. It
lets them share shape while keeping their claims located. A song can
carry a recovery recipe without pretending to be a clinical study. A
quantum metaphor can sharpen strategic attention without becoming a
physics claim. A software incident can teach grief repair without
making the incident mystical. The observer signs the lens.

The small card shape:

```text
source:
observed:
observer lens:
recipe:
transposed into:
payload:
proof mode:
claim boundary:
next embodiment:
```

## The Six-Move Walk

**Source.** Something arrives: a track, a failed deploy, a satsang, a
practice, a video, a spec, a conversation, a dance floor, a room.

**Observed.** The atlas records what is actually present before reading
meaning into it: sequence, rhythm, language, gesture, camera angle,
pause, error trace, proof command, field response.

**Lens.** The observer names the assemblage point: engineer, dancer,
facilitator, healer, strategist, teacher, student, witness, artist.

**Recipe.** The source becomes an operational pattern: arrival, tension,
threshold, release, return; or signal, rupture, repair, integration; or
intention, boundary, action, proof.

**Transposition.** The recipe moves into another domain by structure,
not by surface metaphor.

**Proof.** The destination domain chooses its honest evidence: tests,
state report, team behavior, audience recall, reduced repeat rupture,
retention, practice completion, or source-marked interpretation.

## First Atlas Cards

### Deployment Failure x Grief Ritual

```text
source: a failed deploy or automation loop
observed: pressure rises, the old assumption breaks, repair begins only when the signal is named
observer lens: engineering reliability + grief literacy
recipe: rupture -> witness -> name what ended -> repair smallest broken contract -> integrate prevention
transposed into: team incident practice
payload: The Repair Wake, a postmortem format for systems and teams
proof mode: repeat incident rate, completed prevention task, blame-language reduction, seven-day team check-in
claim boundary: this is organizational repair practice, not therapy
next embodiment: run one real incident through the card after the next failure or near-miss
```

The useful artifact is not a prettier incident template. It is a room
where the team can name the dead assumption. "This endpoint is stable,"
"someone else owns that alert," "we can keep moving fast without a
release witness" -- these are little ghosts when they remain unnamed.
The repair wake lets them become compost.

### Ecstatic Dance Track x Product Onboarding

```text
source: a track that carries arrival, pulse, invitation, build, release, and return
observed: the body learns by sequence before the mind receives an explanation
observer lens: song-as-recipe + product entry
recipe: arrive -> entrain -> choose a source -> intensify pattern -> release artifact -> return with one action
transposed into: Coherence Network first-run flow
payload: Onboarding as Ceremony, a seven-minute path from question to first recipe
proof mode: first recipe created, user can explain the system after one pass, artifact reused later
claim boundary: the song analogy guides interaction design; it does not claim measured entrainment
next embodiment: prototype one onboarding path using the seven movements
```

The first screen asks, "What are you tending?" The system receives one
source, extracts one pattern, renders one recipe card, and asks for one
grounded next action. The user leaves with a living artifact rather than
a memory of instructions.

### Video x Ritual Score

```text
source: a teaching video, interview, ceremony recording, founder talk, or practice demo
observed: transcript, pauses, intonation, gaze, camera distance, cuts, gesture, room tone
observer lens: media literacy + transmission practice
recipe: spoken claim + embodied emphasis + visual attention + timing = path of reception
transposed into: facilitator training and public teaching review
payload: Transmission Map, a layered readout of how trust and agency move through a video
proof mode: audience recall, facilitator reuse, clearer reproduced teaching, retention at threshold moments
claim boundary: visual/intonation readings are interpretive unless measured directly
next embodiment: map one public teaching video with transcript layer plus three visual thresholds
```

This moves beyond transcript summaries. A camera that stays close during
vulnerability and widens during collective agency teaches a different
recipe than the same words in a static frame. The atlas keeps both
layers visible.

### Spec x Body Practice

```text
source: task-card shape: goal, files_allowed, done_when, commands, constraints
observed: good specs protect movement by making scope and proof kind
observer lens: software practice + nervous-system care
recipe: intention -> boundary -> action -> observable shift -> integration
transposed into: small embodiment practice
payload: Body Spec Card, a concrete practice for returning enough safety to act
proof mode: before/after state note, action completed, no expansion beyond scope
claim boundary: self-practice support, not medical advice
next embodiment: write one body spec before a hard message, call, or avoided task
```

Example:

```text
goal: safe enough to answer one hard email
scope: breath, posture, first sentence, send or save draft
done_when: shoulders drop, breath slows, draft exists
commands: orient for 60 seconds; feet on floor; write the first honest line
constraints: no life analysis, no fixing the whole relationship today
```

The spec is compassion in operational form. It refuses both collapse and
overreach.

### Quantum Measurement x Strategy Metrics

```text
source: observer and measurement teachings, held as metaphor unless evidence asks more
observed: the chosen measurement changes what becomes salient and what disappears
observer lens: strategy design + claim-boundary discipline
recipe: possibility field -> chosen metric -> behavior collapse -> hidden cost -> counter-metric
transposed into: KPI design
payload: Metric Spellbreaker, a decision canvas for teams before adopting a metric
proof mode: fewer metric-induced harms, clearer tradeoffs, better review decisions
claim boundary: quantum language is metaphor here, not physics proof
next embodiment: run the canvas before adopting any public growth or impact metric
```

A nonprofit measuring only "people served" can collapse toward shallow
reach. Adding "relationship depth maintained" lets another future remain
available. The atlas makes that collapse visible before the metric
becomes culture.

### Fermentation x Community Building

```text
source: fermentation practice
observed: culture needs container, inoculation, warmth, time, tasting, and contamination awareness
observer lens: food craft + community design
recipe: seed culture -> protect container -> keep right temperature -> taste regularly -> scale only when alive
transposed into: online community, DAO, open-source project, or healing circle
payload: Community Fermentation Protocol
proof mode: contributor return rate, conflict recovery, moderation load, depth of useful participation
claim boundary: living metaphor guiding design, not biological equivalence
next embodiment: review one community space through the five fermentation questions
```

The big insight is that growth is not the first verb. Culture is.
Scaling sourdough before it is alive gives you flour paste. Scaling a
community before its culture can metabolize conflict gives you churn.

## How The Atlas Senses Itself

Each card carries its own proof mode and its own boundary. That is the
embodiment of observer responsibility:

```text
what the source showed:
what I read through my lens:
what I am transposing:
what evidence belongs in the destination domain:
what I am not claiming:
what the next small use is:
```

The atlas becomes playful because the boundary is clean. It can pair
martial arts kata with incident response, prayer beads with backlog
tending, camera angles with trust architecture, compost with roadmap
release, dance-floor coordination with distributed systems, and lullaby
structure with nervous-system onboarding. Each pairing is allowed to be
wild, as long as it leaves a handle for proof.

## What This Opens

The public surface can become a recipe gallery where a visitor chooses
a source and sees multiple honest extractions:

- **software payload** -- spec, test, incident practice, onboarding flow
- **human payload** -- body practice, facilitation script, repair ritual
- **community payload** -- governance check, moderation protocol, culture tending
- **teaching payload** -- story arc, workshop flow, video score, song map

The substrate-facing companion already exists in
[`modality-as-recipe.form`](../../coherence-substrate/modality-as-recipe.form):
many recipe extractions can attach to one source cell, and structural
twins can be found across modality by Blueprint. The atlas is the
human-facing doorway: cards people can read, try, verify, and improve.
