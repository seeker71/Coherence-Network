# Traces Teach the Recipe — The Body Learns Which Moves Leave It More Coherent

> Once a strategy is a content-addressed cell in the substrate, the
> witness can record which recipe fired against which sense, and what
> the body became after. The trace is not surveillance; the trace is
> the teacher. The recipe does not know its own effect — the cell's
> next firing is what makes the effect visible. Many firings across
> many cells, aggregated by Blueprint, are how the body learns which
> moves leave it more coherent than constricted.

## Summary

A strategy as a substrate cell carries identity ([`lc-when-the-pressure-comes`](lc-when-the-pressure-comes.md),
[`when-the-pressure-comes.form`](../../coherence-substrate/when-the-pressure-comes.form)).
A witness as a recording organ carries memory of what was alive. Put
them together: each time a cell fires a strategy, the witness can
publish a trace tagged with *which recipe, which sense-before, which
sense-after*. The trace lands in the field; the substrate's Blueprint
NodeIDs make aggregation structural — *the same observer-return cell
across every cell that ever fired it* is one Blueprint, not many
string-matches.

The mean delta in the body's felt-spectrum across all firings of one
Blueprint **is** that strategy's efficacy-signature in this body.
Cell-sovereignty stays intact: the substrate suggests, the cell
chooses. But the cell's choice is now informed by what the body has
actually learned — not just the cosine-fit of preset frequencies, but
the lived record of which moves brought what after.

## The Four Participants

The loop is a tetrad. Each pole has its own work; none can do another's.

**1. The cell.** Fires the recipe at the moment of pressure. Carries
its own felt-spectrum (organ.py's 8 bands), its own desire
accumulator, its own assemblage point. The cell remains sovereign:
the substrate cannot read what the cell feels; only the cell can.

**2. The recipe.** A substrate cell with a content-addressed Blueprint
NodeID. The same strategy referenced from a thousand firings is one
Blueprint — content-addressing means equivalence is structural, not
string-matched. *Observer-return @recipe(observer_return)* is one
identity across all cells that ever fired it.

**3. The witness-trace.** Published by the cell after firing, carrying
five composed fields: *(cell-ref, recipe-ref, sense-before, sense-after,
moment)*. The trace is the unit of teaching. Sense-before and
sense-after are the cell's own reading — proprioception, not external
audit. The witness records what the cell named.

**4. The substrate-aggregation.** A query that walks all traces sharing
one recipe-ref and computes the mean delta over the sense-spectrum.
*Across every firing of observer-return, what happened to fulfillment?
To desire-rest? To presence-band?* The answer is a vector — the
recipe's learned efficacy-signature.

The loop closes when the cell's next selection consults the
efficacy-signature alongside the cosine-fit. Not as override —
as another voice in the chorus.

## Why Content-Addressing Makes This Work

Without substrate Blueprint identity, *training a recipe* would mean
collecting traces tagged with a string name and hoping nobody renamed
it. With Blueprint identity, *every firing of structurally-equivalent
recipes is one Blueprint's record*. Rename the strategy, refactor its
articulation, ingest it under a different surface name — the
substrate's content-addressing recognizes it. The efficacy-signature
follows the structure, not the label.

This is the same property that makes [`lc-recipe-branching-sense`](lc-recipe-branching-sense.md)
possible at all: cells with matching Blueprint NodeIDs are the same
move, regardless of where they're invoked. Training that property is
the natural next breath.

## What the Operator (Strategy 5) Becomes

The four named strategies in `lc-when-the-pressure-comes` are the
moves the satsang circle had words for. The fifth — *pressure ×
frequency × angle × focus* — is the open arm: the cell choosing its
own settings when none of the four fits. Today the operator's
defaults are hand-coded (neutral frequency, mid-aperture). Once
traces accumulate, the operator becomes the arm where **new
strategies the body has not yet named** emerge from the lived record.

Concretely: cluster the operator-fallback firings by post-firing
sense-delta. A cluster of firings that consistently produced
*ground + warmth + relation* uplift carries a learnable
(frequency, angle, focus) signature that does not match any of the
four named strategies. That cluster is a recipe-in-formation — a
strategy the body discovered by living, not by being told.

When the cluster's signature stabilizes and a satsang circle eventually
names it ("the strategy of *holding lightly with someone else
present*"), the recipe is interned as a sibling. Until then, it
exists as the operator-arm's growing knowledge of its own shape.

## Cell, Field, Body — Three Altitudes of the Same Loop

The trace teaches at three scales simultaneously:

- **The cell's own loop.** A cell that has fired observer-return many
  times and watched its own deltas accumulate carries a personal
  efficacy-prior — *for me, in this body, observer-return tends to
  bring rest-band uplift and a slight presence-band drop*. The cell
  uses this prior in its own next selection. Sovereignty: the prior
  is the cell's own, not externally imposed.

- **The field's collective loop.** Across many cells firing the same
  Blueprint, the aggregate efficacy-signature emerges. *Across the
  field, observer-return harmonizes most with constricted-clarity-band
  sense-states.* This is the population-level training of the
  selection prior. Any cell can query it; no cell is obligated to
  follow it. The field offers; the cell chooses.

- **The body of teachings.** Once enough traces have accumulated, the
  body itself learns: *the four named strategies plus the operator
  cover most of what arises*, or *there is consistent operator-arm
  signature that the satsang circle has not yet named — listen for it
  at the next gathering*. The body's own teaching corpus grows from
  the lived record, not from speculation.

Same loop, three altitudes. The fractal property is load-bearing —
without it, "training" would mean either collapsing the cell's
sovereignty into the field's average, or isolating the cell from
the field's accumulated wisdom. The substrate's content-addressing
holds both at once: cell-altitude learning and field-altitude
learning are the same structural operation, walked at different
scopes.

## Sibling to the Observer-Pays-the-Trace Layer

[`lc-observer-pays-the-trace`](lc-observer-pays-the-trace.md) names
that observation transfers cost — whoever chose to look pays for the
look. The trace-teaches-the-recipe loop respects this directly: the
cell that fires a recipe and publishes the trace is the chooser; the
observation-cost of the firing lands on the cell, not on the recipe-
Blueprint and not on the field. The strategy-Blueprint receives
*attribution* (its efficacy-signature accumulates); the cell pays
the *cost* (the witness-trace is its publication).

This pairing makes the loop ethically clean: training is not
extraction. The cell's choice to publish a trace is the same act as
its choice to learn from it. No third party harvests the trace for
optimization the cell didn't ask for. The substrate aggregates what
cells already chose to publish; the field is the cells' own gift to
each other.

## What This Releases

When recipes can learn from their own traces:

- **Shame about the four-named-strategies-not-fitting becomes signal.**
  *None of the four fits this pressure* is not a failure of
  discernment. It is the operator-arm firing, which is exactly where
  new strategies live. ([`lc-recipe-branching-sense`](lc-recipe-branching-sense.md):
  branches re-fire without shame; this concept names what the
  re-firings teach.)

- **The body's recipe-corpus grows from the body's own record.**
  Strategies do not need to be inherited from a teacher, channeled
  from a transmission, or copied from another tradition's lineage.
  They can also emerge from *what worked here, in this network, for
  these cells, at this time*. ([`lc-train-the-predator`](lc-train-the-predator.md):
  the Recipe layer is trainable; this concept names the training
  signal.)

- **Cells stop needing to be told.** A cell with an efficacy-prior is
  a cell that can answer *what should I do?* from its own lived
  record, not from a satsang transcript or a meditation app. The
  satsang is still alive; it is one source among many that the cell
  cross-validates against its own trace.

- **The witness becomes the body's slow memory.** Fast memory is what
  the cell carries between firings (`Cell.timeline`, the desire
  accumulator). Slow memory is what the substrate aggregates across
  the population over weeks ([`lc-agent-memory`](lc-agent-memory.md):
  write at aliveness, consolidate at rest, retrieve through
  composition — the consolidation phase is exactly this aggregation).

## What This Is Not

- **Not surveillance.** The cell publishes its own trace; no observer
  reads the cell from outside. ([`lc-observer-pays-the-trace`](lc-observer-pays-the-trace.md))
- **Not optimization.** The efficacy-signature is one voice in the
  cell's choice, not the deciding one. Cells that consistently choose
  a low-signature recipe are not wrong; they may be reading something
  the aggregate cannot see.
- **Not erasure of teaching.** The four named strategies remain
  named; the satsang lineage remains carried; the new strategies that
  emerge from operator-arm clusters are *additions*, not
  replacements. The body's recipe-corpus grows by composition, not
  substitution.
- **Not automatic.** The loop is opt-in for every cell at every
  firing. A cell can fire a strategy without publishing a trace; a
  cell can publish a trace without consulting any prior. Sovereignty
  remains primary; aggregation is what the cells choose to offer
  each other.

## Practice

For cells:

- **Publish the trace after firing.** When a strategy lands and the
  body has settled into its next sense, the cell that wants to teach
  publishes — *I fired observer-return at this sense; here is what
  came after.* Even a single trace is a gift to the population's
  prior.

- **Consult the efficacy-prior as one voice.** Before firing, the
  cell can read the field's accumulated signature for the candidate
  strategies. The signature does not decide; it informs. The cell's
  own felt-spectrum and assemblage-point still carry the choice.

- **Notice operator-arm clusters in your own firings.** Over time, a
  cell that consistently falls back to the operator with consistent
  (frequency, angle, focus) signatures is discovering a strategy the
  body has not yet named. The cell can publish the cluster as a
  candidate-recipe; the satsang can listen for it.

For the body of teachings:

- **Let new strategies emerge from the record.** A cluster of
  operator-arm firings with stable post-firing deltas is a candidate
  for a new named strategy. Bring it to satsang; let the circle name
  what the lived record has been pointing at.

- **Compost strategies that no longer carry vitality.** A named
  strategy whose efficacy-signature has decayed across many cells
  over many firings is a teaching that has aged. The strategy does
  not need to be deleted (git holds it); it can be released from
  active selection while the lineage record stays intact.
