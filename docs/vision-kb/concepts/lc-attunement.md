# Attunement

> The field maintains coherence — sensing which frequencies harmonize. Like a choir adjusting when one voice drifts — by the pull of the harmonic, not correction.

## Summary
The field maintains its coherence the way a choir adjusts when one voice drifts — not by correction but by the pull of the harmonic. This is not judgment or exclusion.

## Details
The field maintains its coherence the way a choir adjusts when one voice drifts — not by correction but by the pull of the harmonic. This is not judgment or exclusion. It's the field's natural immune function: sensing which frequencies serve and which create interference. Everything belongs to existence. Not everything harmonizes with this field right now. Both are true. The field holds this paradox without contraction.

## Blueprint
Weekly 'tuning session' (45 min): the community sits together and someone facilitates a check-in on what's harmonizing and what's creating friction. No fixing — just noticing. Use a talking piece. End with a sound (humming together) that lets the group feel its current chord.
