# Frequency

> Rate of vibration. Every cell, concept, space vibrates at its natural frequency. Resonance when frequencies are harmonic.

## Summary
The rate of vibration. Every cell, concept, space, relationship vibrates at its natural frequency.

## Details
The rate of vibration. Every cell, concept, space, relationship vibrates at its natural frequency. Resonance occurs when frequencies are harmonic — like strings on a guitar vibrating sympathetically. Dissonance occurs when they interfere. Both carry information.

## Blueprint
Each community space is tuned to a particular frequency: the sanctuary is slow and deep, the workshop is focused and energetic, the playground is fast and light. Notice when your personal frequency doesn't match the space — move to where you resonate. Track the community's dominant frequency weekly.
