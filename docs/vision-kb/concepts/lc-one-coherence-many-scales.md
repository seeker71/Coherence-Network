## The ladder — what is measured, what is welcomed

The same coupling recipe (and the substrate's content-addressing beneath it)
runs at every rung. Holding the embrace turn precisely: what is **measured** is
embraced exactly; what is **reported but not yet measurable** is welcomed with
open curiosity and named at source-marked distance — neither dismissed nor
asserted as fact.

- **Quantum physics — measured.** Energy levels are discrete; systems cohere and
  decohere; coupled quantum oscillators lock. Quantum coherence is the substrate
  of the laser and, increasingly evidenced, of photosynthesis.
- **The structure of matter — measured.** Atoms are a nucleus and electron
  shells filling by capacity (2, 8, 18, 32 = 2n²); an element *is* its
  shell-filling. Noble gases are a closed-shell equivalence class — content-
  addressing in the periodic table.
- **Biochemistry — measured.** Molecules compose from atoms by bonds; the genetic
  code translates codons to amino acids ([`lc-the-genetic-code`](lc-the-genetic-code.md)).
- **Bioelectricity — measured.** Cells hold a membrane voltage from ion
  gradients; tissues compute with bioelectric patterns that instruct form
  ([`lc-bioelectric-pattern`](lc-bioelectric-pattern.md), Levin/Tufts). A gradient
  drives a flux — the same shape as nutrient flow and photosynthesis below.
- **Biomagnetism — measured.** The heart generates the body's largest
  electromagnetic field, detectable several feet away (magnetocardiography;
  HeartMath). Its field can entrain — couple to — another body's rhythms.
- **Photosynthesis — measured.** A photon lifts an electron; an electron-
  transport chain (a redox *gradient → flux*) stores the light as chemical
  energy. Light becomes matter-bound energy by a cascade of couplings.
- **Nutrient flow — measured.** Source → transport → sink; a concentration
  gradient drives movement (xylem, phloem, the gut, the bloodstream). The same
  *gradient → flux* recipe as bioelectricity and the electron-transport chain.
- **The crystalline ↔ silicon bridge — measured.** A crystal is a periodic
  lattice; silicon's semiconducting band structure *arises from* that

  [`lc-global-workspace`](lc-global-workspace.md)). Neural binding *is* coherence
  — phase-locking across regions.
- **Heart-brain coherence — measured.** Heart-rate variability and brain rhythms

  leads* is HeartMath's framing, held with care.
