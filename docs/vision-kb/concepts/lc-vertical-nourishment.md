# Vertical Nourishment — Receiving Directly From Source

> The fullness that does not depend on people behaving well, on
> circumstances cooperating, on validation arriving on time. The
> vertical line — body, breath, deeper self, source — that fills
> the system from within and makes everything else optional.
>
> *Source-marked from Dr. Sue Morter, "From Drained To Nourished,"
> April 2026.*

## Summary

Vertical nourishment names the pattern of receiving directly from
the inner axis — body, breath, deeper self, source — rather than
trying to extract sustenance from people and circumstances. When a
person is vertically nourished, their relationships become places
of overflow and exchange rather than places of leakage and seeking.

## Details

The horizontal economy of getting-from-others is the pattern most
of modern life teaches. Get love from your partner. Get
recognition from your work. Get safety from your bank account.
Get joy from achievements. The system keeps reaching outward,
trying to fill an inner space the outward will never quite reach.

The vertical pattern is different. The fullness arrives from
inside, through:

- **Breath** — slow, embodied, full breaths that drop the system
  out of survival mode and into receiving.
- **Body** — actual sensation, actual aliveness, the warmth of
  presence in tissue.
- **Deeper self** — the awareness underneath personality that has
  always been here.
- **Source** (whatever name fits the cell — divine, the field,
  Tao, presence) — the larger-than-personal vitality the body
  participates in.

The transmission frames this as how the system is **designed**.
Joy is not earned through accomplishments; it is accessed through
alignment with the inner architecture. The experience of *trying
hard to get joy* is itself the obstruction to noticing joy is
already here.

When a person is vertically nourished, horizontal interactions
shift. They give from overflow rather than from depletion. They
receive without needing to receive. Their boundaries clarify
because their needs no longer require any specific other person to
meet them.

## Practice

- Several times a day, drop attention into the body. Hand on
  heart. Breath lower than usual. Feel what is here.
- Notice when the system is reaching outward for something. Pause.
  Ask: *can I receive this from within?*
- Trust that vertical fullness is not an achievement; it is what
  remains when horizontal seeking quiets.
