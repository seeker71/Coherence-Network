## Blueprint
Open-source everything. Community tech team maintains shared devices: sensors for garden and water systems, communication tools, energy monitoring. Technology budget prioritizes tools that extend sensing (soil sensors, weather stations) over tools that replace human connection. All data is community-owned.

## Materials & Methods
- **Soil moisture sensors (Arduino)** — $5-15 per sensor, ESP32 microcontroller + WiFi. Monitor garden moisture remotely. Open-source firmware.
- **Weather station** — DIY from off-the-shelf sensors (temp, humidity, wind, rain, UV). $50-200. Data logged to community dashboard.
- **Energy monitoring** — current transformers on main panels, real-time display. $100-500 for whole-community monitoring.
- **Water level sensors** — cistern and pond levels, automated alerts. $20-50 per sensor.
- **Community mesh network** — LoRa or WiFi mesh for sensor data across property. $200-1,000 for 50-acre coverage.
- **Maker space / fab lab** — 3D printer, CNC router, hand tools, electronics bench. $5,000-20,000 core setup.

## At Scale
- **50 people**: 1 maker space (400 sqft), 10-20 garden/water/energy sensors, community mesh network, shared laptop station, 1-2 tech-trained members.
- **100 people**: Dedicated tech workshop (800 sqft), full sensor grid, 3D printing, CNC capability, 3-4 tech members, training program for all residents.
- **200 people**: Full fab lab (1500 sqft), IoT sensor network across all systems, community intranet, repair cafe, tech apprenticeship, sharing tools with neighboring communities.

## Climate Adaptations
- **Temperate** — weather station critical for frost warnings, greenhouse automation (vents, watering), snow-load monitoring on roofs
- **Tropical** — humidity and mold monitoring, solar panel cleaning systems, rainwater harvesting automation, mosquito habitat tracking
- **Arid** — water level monitoring paramount, drip irrigation automation, dust sensors for solar panel maintenance, evaporation tracking
- **Alpine** — avalanche risk monitoring, snow-melt stream flow gauging, greenhouse climate control, backup power for winter
