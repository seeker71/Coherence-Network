## Details
Every channel: words, presence, touch, silence, rhythm, movement. At higher frequencies, communication is primarily energetic — words carry a small fraction of the actual signal. Honest signal is a vitality practice. When a cell transmits 'fine' while feeling contracted, the corrupted signal degrades the whole field's sensing. The field develops sensitivity to the full spectrum of transmission.

## Blueprint
Practice 'clean signal' in all community communication: say what you mean, mean what you say, notice when you're performing. Weekly 'Forum' process (ZEGG-style) where individuals express authentically and the community witnesses. No gossip protocol — if you have something to say about someone, say it to them first.

## Living Examples
- **Dog communication** — body posture, ear position, tail movement, scent carry 90% of the message
- **Orchestra conductor** — communicating tempo, dynamics, emotion through gesture alone
- **Silence between close friends** — more is communicated in the quiet than in words
- **Forest chemical signaling** — trees warn neighbors of pest attacks through volatile organic compounds

## Aligned Places
- **Quaker meeting houses** (Global (originated England)) — Silence until moved to speak — communication stripped to essence, no clergy, no ritual, only honest signal.
- **Aboriginal message sticks** (Australia) — Carved wooden objects carrying encoded messages between nations — transmission as sacred responsibility across vast distances.
- **Toastmasters International** (Global) — Practice-based approach to honest, clear communication — learning to transmit with integrity in community.
