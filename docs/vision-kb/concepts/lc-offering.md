# Offering

> What flows from natural frequency. Heart beats because that's its nature. Bird sings at dawn because dawn + bird = song.

## Summary
What flows from a cell's natural frequency. Heart beats because that's its nature.

## Details
What flows from a cell's natural frequency. Heart beats because that's its nature. The bird sings at dawn not from obligation but because dawn + bird = song. Every cell's fullest expression IS its offering to the field. There is no distinction between self-expression and service — when a cell is fully alive, both are the same movement.

## Blueprint
No assigned roles. A 'resonance board' where the community posts what it needs; people step forward from resonance, not obligation. Weekly 'offering circle' where anyone can share what they want to contribute. Track offerings, not hours — what did you give that was alive?

## Living Examples
- **Dawn bird chorus** — each species singing its frequency, the forest symphony emerges
- **A master carpenter finishing a joint** — the precision IS the offering, the beauty IS the function
- **A grandmother telling stories** — the transmission IS the gift, effortless because it's her nature
- **Bees pollinating** — they feed themselves AND propagate the ecosystem, one movement

## Aligned Places
- **Mondragon cooperatives** (Basque Country, Spain) — 80,000 worker-owners. Each person's offering valued not by market but by contribution to the whole.

## How It Fits
Offering is the Expressing flow as service. It replaces the concept of 'work' — there is no work, only expression that happens to nourish the field.
