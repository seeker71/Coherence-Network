# Field Edge

> Semi-permeable, alive. Like cell membrane: selectively permeable by resonance. Open because it trusts attunement. Healthy field radiates.

## Summary
Semi-permeable, alive. Like a cell membrane: selectively permeable based on molecular resonance.

## Details
Semi-permeable, alive. Like a cell membrane: selectively permeable based on molecular resonance. The field is open because it trusts its own attunement. A wall is built from fear. A membrane is built from self-knowledge. Generous — a healthy field radiates its vitality outward, nourishing the surrounding environment.

## Blueprint
Designate a 'flowing edge' zone with guest accommodations — yurts, tents, simple rooms. Visitors stay 1-3 weeks before any joining conversation. The membrane is alive: some seasons the community opens wide, others it contracts to integrate. A 'welcome team' of 2-3 people greets and orients every visitor.

## Living Examples
- **Cell membrane** — lets water and nutrients through, blocks toxins, continuously adapting
- **Skin** — semi-permeable, breathes, senses, protects, heals itself, beautiful
- **Village commons in traditional societies** — shared space open to all, managed by community sensing
- **A garden hedge** — defines space without walling, invites looking, hosts wildlife

## Aligned Places
- **Marinaleda** (Andalusia, Spain) — Village that rebuilt itself as a cooperative with open-door policy — anyone can come, contribute, and become part of the field.
- **Riace** (Calabria, Italy) — Dying village revived by welcoming refugees — the membrane opened and the field came alive with new energy and diversity.
- **Arakmbut communities** (Madre de Dios, Peru) — Indigenous communities with clear protocols for welcoming outsiders — the membrane is alive, permeable but discerning.

## How It Fits
Field Edge is the field's membrane — where it meets the world. It enables The Network (connecting with other fields), Joining (new cells entering), and the relationship with Land.
