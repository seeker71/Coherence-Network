# Sensing

> The field feels itself continuously. Every cell transmits and receives. Spanda — the vibration connecting everything. Like dolphins sensing each other simultaneously without effort.

## Summary
The field's nervous system. Every cell transmits its actual state and receives others'.

## Details
The field's nervous system. Every cell transmits its actual state and receives others'. This is not reporting or meetings — it's the continuous background awareness a healthy body has of itself. Proprioception at the collective level. The tantric traditions call this spanda — the subtle vibration of aliveness connecting everything.

## Blueprint
Install a daily sensing round (5 minutes, morning circle): each person shares one sentence of what's actually alive in them — not plans, not performance, just signal. Rotate a 'field reader' role weekly — someone who pays extra attention to what the collective is feeling but not saying.

## Living Examples
- **Pod of dolphins** — each member sensing position, speed, emotional state of others simultaneously, without effort
- **Your body right now** — you know your temperature, your hunger, your energy level without checking instruments
- **Indigenous talking circle** — the stick passes, each voice is heard, the circle holds the whole

## Aligned Places

## How It Fits
Sensing is the nervous system — without it, no other system can function. It enables the field intelligence that replaces governance, the circulation that replaces economics, and the attunement that replaces law.
