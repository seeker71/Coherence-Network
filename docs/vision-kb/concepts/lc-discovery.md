## Summary

## Details
Always learning through immersion. The way a wolf pup learns to hunt by being with the pack — no instruction, just participation in what's alive. Play is the primary discovery mode. The field's collective intelligence is accessible through relationship, not through courses. Mastery radiates naturally from cells that have developed depth.

## Blueprint
No curriculum. Apprenticeship model: learn by doing alongside someone experienced. Community 'skill shares' monthly — anyone can teach anything. Children learn by participating in real adult activities, not simulated ones. Library of tools, seeds, books, and instruments open to all.
