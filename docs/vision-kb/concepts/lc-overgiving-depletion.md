# Overgiving Depletion — The Leak in the Helping Pattern

> Giving without receiving creates a slow leak. The leak is not
> closed by withholding from those you give to; it is closed by
> opening the vertical channel so the giving comes from overflow
> rather than from one's own reserves.
>
> *Source-marked from Dr. Sue Morter, "From Drained To Nourished,"
> April 2026.*

## Summary

Overgiving is the pattern of consistently outflowing more than is
inflowing — to a partner, to family, to clients, to a community,
to a cause. The transmission names this as a leak. Crucially, it
does not prescribe withholding from the receivers. It prescribes
opening the vertical channel so the giver fills from within while
continuing to give.

## Details

Overgivers often arrive at their pattern through real care, real
gifts, real desire to help. The harm is not in the giving; the
harm is in the *source* of the giving. When the source is the
giver's reserves — their energy, attention, time, money, emotional
capacity — and the reserves are not refilling vertically, the
giving slowly empties them.

Common signs the leak is happening:

- Resentment grows toward the people being given to, even when
  the giver consciously wants to give.
- The giver's own life shrinks; their hobbies, friendships, and
  joys quietly disappear.
- Exhaustion that sleep does not fix.
- A sense that one is performing the role of *helper* or *fixer*
  rather than expressing genuine care.

The transmission's reframe: receiving does not have to come from
the same people one gives to. The vertical channel — breath, body,
deeper self, source — is the primary refill. Horizontal reciprocity
becomes lovely bonus rather than survival necessity.

Once the vertical channel opens, several things happen at once:

- The giver gives from overflow rather than from depletion.
- Recipients can receive without the burden of being someone's
  primary nourishment.
- Resentment quiets without effort.
- The giver can see clearly which dynamics are actually serving
  growth and which are running on fumes — and can adjust without
  drama.

## Practice

- Audit one giving relationship honestly. Is the vertical channel
  open during this giving, or am I running on reserves?
- Before giving, fill vertically. Breath. Heart. Presence.
  Several minutes if needed. Then give from overflow.
- Resist the urge to "fix" the imbalance by demanding more from
  recipients. The fix is upstream of them.
