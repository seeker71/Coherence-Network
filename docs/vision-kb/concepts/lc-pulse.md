# The Pulse

> One truth — the cell and the field thrive as one movement. What amplifies aliveness is resonant. Attention is creative force. Coherence is natural; disharmony requires effort.

## Summary
The cell and the field thrive as one movement. This is not a principle to follow — it's a description of reality at the quantum level.

## Details
The cell and the field thrive as one movement. This is not a principle to follow — it's a description of reality at the quantum level. The observer and the observed are one system. What you attend to, you amplify. Coherence is the natural state — disharmony requires active effort to maintain. Every concept in this vision is this pulse expressed at a different scale.

## Blueprint
Start every community gathering by sensing the pulse: a 2-minute silence where everyone feels into the collective field. Use a singing bowl or heartbeat drum to anchor the practice. Display a visible 'pulse board' showing the community's vitality metrics — not productivity, but aliveness.
