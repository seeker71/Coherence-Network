# What If You Actually Owned Your Work?

## The Problem You Already Know

You make things. Videos, music, art, writing, courses, designs, blueprints, guides. You pour hours into them. And then:

- **YouTube** takes 45% and decides who sees it. One algorithm change and your income drops 60% overnight. Happened in 2017 (Adpocalypse), 2022 (Shorts pivot), 2025 (AI summaries). It'll happen again.
- **Instagram** shows your post to 3% of your followers. Want the rest? Pay. Your content trains their AI. Your engagement data sells ads. You earn $0 unless a brand picks you.
- **TikTok** gives you a Creator Fund that pays $0.02-0.04 per 1,000 views. For a video that took you 8 hours. That's not a creator economy. That's digital sharecropping.
- **Spotify** pays $0.003-0.005 per stream. A song you spent months on earns you a coffee after 1,000 plays.
- **Patreon** is better — your audience pays you directly. But Patreon takes 5-12%, Stripe takes 3%, and you're building on someone else's platform. If Patreon changes terms, you adapt or die.
- **Substack** takes 10%. Better than most. But your growth depends on their recommendation algorithm, and you're locked into their format.

Every platform is the same deal: **you create, they distribute, they take a cut, they control who sees your work, and they can change the rules whenever they want.**

## What If It Worked Differently

Imagine this:

You make a video about building raised garden beds. You post it on YouTube like always — that's where your audience is. But you also register it here. Takes 30 seconds. You tag it with what it's about (gardening, food growing, DIY).

Someone watches it through a link on our platform. They don't pay. Viewing is free, always. But their view is tracked — tied to what they care about.

Six months later, that viewer creates their own content — a timelapse of the garden they built using your technique. Their creation generates value in the network. And because your video is in their history — because it's part of what shaped their contribution — **a portion of their earnings flows back to you**.

Not from ads. Not from subscriptions. From resonance. Your work inspired their work. The network noticed. Value flowed back.

And here's the thing: **you can verify every cent.** It's not a trust-me promise. It's cryptographic math. Public API. Anyone can check.

## How It Actually Works (No Jargon)

**Step 1: Keep doing what you do.** Post to YouTube, Instagram, wherever. Don't change anything about your existing workflow.

**Step 2: Register your content.** Link your videos, articles, designs, music, courses. Each one gets a permanent fingerprint (a hash) that proves it's yours. Think of it like timestamping + watermarking, but on a public ledger.

**Step 3: Add a tracking link.** In your video description or bio, add a link like:
```
🌱 More on growing food: coherencycoin.com/r/yourname/nourishment
```
Every click through that link is a tracked read. Your content resonates through our network even though it lives on YouTube.

**Step 4: Your profile builds automatically.** The more you create and the more people engage with it, the clearer your frequency profile becomes. Think of it like Spotify Wrapped, but for your creative DNA — what you make, what you care about, what resonates with your audience.

**Step 5: You find your people.** Other creators with similar profiles become visible. Not because an algorithm recommends them. Because your creative frequencies align. A cluster of permaculture YouTubers. A network of meditation teachers. A group of open-source hardware designers.

**Step 6: You form a community and set YOUR rules.** This is the part that doesn't exist anywhere else. When 3-5 resonant creators come together, you form a community and **design your own earning algorithm**:

- How much does original content earn vs. remixes?
- How much does real-world implementation earn vs. tutorials?
- What percentage flows back through reading history?
- How is hosting infrastructure rewarded?

**You decide.** Not YouTube. Not a VC. Not an algorithm you can't see. You and the people you resonate with.

## The Money Part (Let's Be Real)

"Sounds great, but can I pay rent with this?"

Honestly? Not today. The network is early. CC (Coherence Credits) are an internal currency. As the network grows:

1. **Now**: CC tracks your contribution and builds your reputation in the network
2. **Soon**: CC exchanges between communities — your permaculture CC buys a woodworker's time
3. **Next**: CC converts to USDC (a stablecoin pegged to USD) via a treasury-backed exchange
4. **Eventually**: CC converts to real money in your bank account

The exchange rate is transparent: treasury reserves ÷ total CC in circulation. No speculation. No pump and dump. Just math.

But here's what you get **right now** that no platform gives you:

- **An algorithm you shaped.** Not one imposed on you.
