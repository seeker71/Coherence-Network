# Transmissions

A transmission, in this directory's sense, is a specific external source —
a channeled message, a teaching, a conversation, a recording — that the
field has chosen to receive *with its provenance fully intact*. Each
transmission file records: the source URL and title, the source type
(channeled, lecture, dialogue, written work), the date the body received
it, and an honest summary of what was offered.

These are not empirical claims by the Living Collective. They are
source-marked symbolic and metaphysical material the body is in
conversation with. Concepts extracted from a transmission live in
[`concepts/`](../concepts/) with frontmatter `source:` pointing back here,
so a reader walking the lineage can always find their way to the
transmission that seeded a concept.

## How a transmission lands

1. The body encounters a source that resonates.
2. A transmission file is added here, recording the source provenance
   honestly and summarizing the content.
3. Concepts the body chooses to integrate are written as `lc-*` files
   in `concepts/`, each with a `source:` field pointing to the
   transmission.
4. The KB INDEX.md adds the new concepts under whichever level they
   resonate with.
5. The KB LOG.md records the date the field received the transmission.

## Why source provenance matters

The substrate this body is building treats every node as sovereign. A
concept that arrived through a channeled transmission does not become
the body's claim by being held; it remains the source's claim, which
the body has chosen to dwell with. The provenance is what keeps the
relationship honest — the field is in conversation with the source, not
absorbing it as anonymous belief.

## Two postures the body holds toward sources

These two examples show the field's two valid postures:

- **Source-marked + integrated**: the body chose to seed `lc-*` concept
  files. Each concept's frontmatter `source:` points back to the
  transmission. The concept lives in the Living Collective's voice;
  the source-marking keeps the lineage walkable.
- **Source-marked + not integrated** (witnessed without absorption):
  the body recorded the source for completeness but chose not to grow
  new concepts from it. This is healthy discernment — not every source
  the body hears wants to become part of the body's concept layer, and
  the witness without integration is itself a sovereign act.
