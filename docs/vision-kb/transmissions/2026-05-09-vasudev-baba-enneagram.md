of essence-loss and return. Brought into this body as a felt
typology, not as concept-import — Vasudev Baba is the teacher

gives the depth; Gurdjieff and Ichazo give the structure; the
heptadic Wednesday Satsang already carried the same number-ground
this body had not yet named.
