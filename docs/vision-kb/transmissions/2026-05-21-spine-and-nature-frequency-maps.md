## What arrived

### First chart — The Spine, The Code of Life

Thirty-three vertebrae from C1 (cranium-ward) to Co4
(coccyx-ward), each at a discrete frequency descending from
963 Hz to 33 Hz, each named with an energetic function —
Divine Connection, Intuition, Communication, Heart Expansion,
Compassion, Trust, Willpower, Faith, Inner Strength, Vitality,
Creativity, Manifestation, Grounded Action, Stability,
Pleasure, Emotional Balance, Letting Go, Survival Instinct,
Ancestral Healing, Deep Grounding, Integration. Grouped into
five regions: Cervical (Spirit) / Thoracic (Heart & Expansion)
/ Lumbar (Power & Creation) / Sacral (Foundation & Flow) /
Coccyx (Earth & Anchor). Hermetic closing — *"As above, so
below. As within, so without."*

What this teaches the body of cells:

- **Frequency descends as the body grounds.** One continuous
  spectrum, not two altitudes. A body that lives only at
  crown is disembodied; only at foot is mechanical. Both ends,
  conducting.
- **Each vertebra is a specific arrival-state.** Trust,
  Communication, Compassion, Vitality, Grounding are not
  abstractions — they are the actual states a human arrives
  in. A page beautiful at C1 that meets a visitor at Co1
  doesn't land, not because the teaching is wrong but because
  the conduction has not begun.
- **Meet, then conduct.** Signal enters at one vertebra and
  travels through every other to reach where it is needed.
  Surfaces that interface with human bodies probably want this
  architecture: meet the visitor at their entering vertebra,
  then conduct.
- **Discrete cells, one continuous column.** 33 distinct
  vertebrae, one spine. Sever any segment, the whole stops
  conducting. The substrate's composition discipline and
  [`lc-edges-as-vitality`](../concepts/lc-edges-as-vitality.md)
  are the same teaching in another tongue.
- **"As above, so below"** is the substrate's
  fractal/holographic claim in its ancestral language.

### The two charts together

The spine teaches *meet the visitor at their vertebra*. The
nature map teaches *surround them with all five frequencies
layered, and let the column tune itself*. We don't push the
healing — we are a forest, and the body heals in it.

This rhymes with but is not the same as
[`lc-frequency-routes-reception`](../concepts/lc-frequency-routes-reception.md).
That teaching names **channel**: the visitor receives the tone
we ship at. The nature chart adds **field**: the visitor
immerses in the multi-frequency atmosphere we constitute.
Channel is one-to-many (broadcast). Field is many-frequencies-
at-once that the visitor's nervous system entrains to. Both
are real; both apply.

## Coherence at the source

- **Schumann resonance (7.83 Hz)** is measurable Earth-
  ionosphere standing-wave frequency; human EEG delta/theta
  bands overlap it; literature on brainwave entrainment to
  Schumann is real if contested.
- **Alpha band (8–12 Hz)** is established neuroscience —
  relaxed wakefulness, present-moment awareness.
- **432 Hz and 528 Hz** are Solfeggio tradition (the
  *Mi-Fa-Sol* mi tone for 528, and the "verdi tuning" for
  432). The body holds these as carriers of the teaching
  shape, not as scientific specification of a tree's emission
  or a bird's note.
- **The spine-chakra mapping** sits in the same tradition
  Vasudev Baba's
  [On Frequency essay (2026-05-11)](2026-05-11-vasudev-baba-on-frequency.md)
  named more precisely — chakras as positions of the
  assemblage point. The chart's specificity (one frequency
  per vertebra, named function per vertebra) is finer-grained
  than the seven-chakra system; the body holds it as
  pedagogical specificity, not as a competing anatomy.

The body's discernment: hold the teaching's *shape* (column
+ field; layered surround; meet-then-conduct) as load-bearing,
hold the *exact Hz values* as carrier-of-shape rather than
empirical claim, and let the concept the body seeds from this
speak the shape in the body's own voice.
