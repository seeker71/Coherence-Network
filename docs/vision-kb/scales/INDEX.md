# Scales — 50 / 100 / 200 People

> The same organism at different magnifications.

**Narrative companion**: [organism-stages.md](organism-stages.md) — the lived experience of each scale (Seed 8-15, Sapling 30-50, Tree 80-150, Grove 150-300, Network 5-20 fields), with character portraits and real-world echoes. Read for felt sense; read this file for numbers.

## 50 People — Seed Community

**The feel**: Everyone knows everyone. Meals together every day. One fire circle holds all. Intimate, transparent, no one invisible.

| Aspect | Size/Count | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Land | 10-30 acres | 5 cultivated, rest wild |
| Dwellings | 15-20 units | Mix of singles, couples, families |
| Common kitchen | 1 | Seats 50, daily shared dinner |
| Garden | 2-5 acres | Food forest + annual beds |
| Workshop | 1 shared | 200-400 sqft |
| Gathering circle | 1 | Seats all 50 |
| Children | 5-15 | Integrated, no separate "school" |
| Elders | 3-8 | Central to decision-making |
| Monthly cost/person | $800-1500 | Including food, shared expenses |

**Governance**: Daily sensing rounds. Weekly circle. All voices heard directly. No delegation needed.

**Cost range**: $1.3M-$5M total / $26K-100K per person
**Timeline**: 12-24 months to livable

## 100 People — Full Community

**The feel**: Two neighborhoods of ~50, each with its own rhythm. Everyone still recognizable. Enough diversity for specialization — dedicated musicians, builders, growers, healers.

| Aspect | Size/Count | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Land | 30-80 acres | 15 cultivated, rest wild/forest |
| Dwellings | 30-40 units | Clustered in 2 neighborhoods |
| Common kitchens | 2 | One per neighborhood + 1 festival kitchen |
| Garden | 5-15 acres | Established food forest + market garden |
| Workshop | 2-3 | Woodwork, metal, pottery/craft |
| Gathering circles | 2 | Neighborhood + full-community |
| Children | 10-30 | Nature school, multiple age groups |
| Elders | 8-15 | Elder council with rotating facilitation |
| Monthly cost/person | $600-1200 | Economies of scale |

**Governance**: Neighborhood circles weekly. Full community circle monthly. Working groups for specific domains. Elder council for deep matters.

**Cost range**: $2.8M-$11M total / $28K-110K per person
**Timeline**: 18-36 months to livable

---

## 200 People — Network Node

**The feel**: A village. Four neighborhoods. Specialists in every domain. Children's school with multiple teachers. Visitors regularly. Connected to other communities. Can host gatherings of 500+.

| Aspect | Size/Count | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Land | 80-200 acres | 30 cultivated, trails, wild corridors |
| Dwellings | 60-80 units | 4 neighborhoods of ~50 |
| Common kitchens | 4 | One per neighborhood + 1 festival |
| Garden | 15-30 acres | Commercial-scale food forest |
| Workshop | 5+ | Full maker space + studio spaces |
| Gathering circles | 5 | 4 neighborhood + 1 amphitheater |
| Children | 25-60 | Full nature school, apprenticeships |
| Elders | 15-30 | Elder council + wisdom keepers |
| Monthly cost/person | $500-1000 | Significant economies of scale |

**Governance**: Neighborhood autonomy with village coordination. Specialized circles (food, building, ceremony, health). Festival council quarterly.

**Cost range**: $5.5M-$22M total / $28K-110K per person
**Timeline**: 24-48 months to livable

---

## Scaling Principle

The community doesn't "grow" — it **replicates**. When a neighborhood reaches ~50, a new one buds. When the village reaches ~200, a new village buds. Connected by the network (lc-network), sharing knowledge, seeds, songs, people.
