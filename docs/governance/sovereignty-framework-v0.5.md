### 4. Trust in Joy & Discernment

I follow what feels **alive**, **joyful**, and in resonance with my current frequency.

### 8. Harmony Over Conflict

All contrasts, conflicts, and challenges — whether personal, legal, financial, or systemic — are viewed as opportunities to convert dissonance into higher harmony.

I choose creation over defense, alignment over opposition, and long-term coherence over short-term victory.
