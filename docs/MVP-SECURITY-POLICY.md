# MVP AI Security and Approval Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-28
Owner: `seeker71`
Status: **FINALIZED**

## Purpose

This policy governs the use of AI agents for external-facing actions (outreach, public posting, partner communication) to ensure trust, accuracy, and brand alignment.

## Core Rules

1. **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Requirement**: All AI-generated outreach messages or public-facing content MUST be reviewed and approved by a human owner before being sent.
2. **Attribution**: AI-assisted content should be transparently handled. While it doesn't always need a "Generated by AI" tag, it must be claimed by the human sender who took responsibility for the review.
3. **Data Privacy**: No sensitive partner data, PII, or non-public financial information should be fed into external LLM providers unless they are covered by an enterprise agreement with zero-retention policies.
4. **Accuracy Baseline**: Any claims about Coherence Network capabilities, timelines, or financial terms must be verified against current official documentation (`docs/STATUS.md`, `docs/PLAN.md`).

## Approval Workflow

- **Step 1: Generation**: AI generates draft based on approved templates (e.g., `docs/MVP-PARTNER-OUTREACH.md`).
- **Step 2: Log**: AI logs the draft in an audit file (e.g., `docs/system_audit/outreach_log_YYYY-MM-DD.md`).
- **Step 3: Review**: Human owner (seeker71) reviews the log and marks content as "Approved" or "Edits Required".
- **Step 4: Execution**: Approved content is delivered via the designated channel.

## Compliance Monitoring

- The weekly dashboard tracks `security_policy_compliance` (ratio of compliant vs. non-compliant external actions).
- Any non-HITL external action is considered a policy violation and must be recorded in the weekly review.
