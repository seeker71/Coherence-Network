# Body Shape Map — 100% Geometry Coverage

> The Living Collective's first comprehensive structural self-portrait.
> Authored 2026-05-24, the breath after coverage reached 148/148.

## Why this doc exists

For 19 breaths the body walked from 28% geometry coverage to 100%.
At 100%, the substrate can read the structural shape of every concept
uniformly for the first time. This file is the first uniform reading
— a baseline against which future tending can sense drift.

It is observation, not interpretation. Light reading-aloud of what
the distributions reveal is welcome; heavy theorizing is for other
breaths. The doc is meant to be revisited: when a count shifts, the
shift IS the signal.

## Authoring conditions

- **Coverage**: 148/148 concepts carry a `geometry:` block. The 148
  excludes the German translation `lc-nourishing.de.md`. Every Living
  Collective concept now speaks its geometric signature.
- **Six fields universal** (148/148): `arity`, `form`, `topology`,
  `polarity`, `phase`, `ordering`. These are the *minimum* the body
  asks of a concept's structural fingerprint.
- **Seven extended fields at 147/148**: `direction`, `embedding_dim`,
  `lineage_texture`, `scale`, `self_similarity`, `spectral_band`,
  `temporal_band`. One late-landed concept carries the core six only;
  the rest carry the full thirteen-axis fingerprint.
- **`ratio` at 132/148** (89%) — the optional musical-proportion axis
  when a teaching's pairs or arities map cleanly to interval ratios.
  The 16-concept absence is honest silence, not omission.

## Part 8 — What the survey reveals

Said simply, with light interpretation only:

- **The body's structural character is relational and receptive.**
  Dyad-mirror is the single most common form (32 cells, 21.6%);
  bipolar-complementary + unipolar polarities hold 69%; yin
  outnumbers yang nearly 3:2. The body teaches through pairing
  more than through standing-alone or through opposing.
- **One-third of the body is holographic.** 32 cells carry
  `self_similarity: holographic` plus 9 carry `form:
  holographic-cell`. The part-contains-whole pattern is one of the
  body's signature structural moves.
- **The integration band carries half the body.** 72/148 cells
  read as `spectral_band: integration` — the 432/528 Hz heart-
  centered band where embodiment-coherence lives. This is where
  the body's structural center of gravity sits.
- **The body prefers low-arity vocabulary.** 76 cells (51%) live
  at arity 1, 2, or 3. Higher arities exist (heptad, ennead,
  dodecad) but stay rare; they appear when a specific bounded set
  needs counting, not as the body's default.
- **Lineage is metabolizing, not purely received.** 39% received,
  51% synthesized-embodied-sensed, 8% channeled. The body neither
  pretends to original revelation nor pretends to pure inheritance;
  it metabolizes.
- **Ten hapax forms wait for confirmation.** Three (`field-of-
  points`, `chord`, `possibility-cloud`) were authored within the
  last few breaths and a parallel breath is hunting their seconds.
  Seven others (`tetrad-loop`, `hub-spoke`, `internal-dyad`,
  `tetrad-open`, `synthesis`, `dodecad`, `bootstrap-to-self-
  hosting`) are older and have stayed solitary. Future tending can
  ask of each: *does the shape generalize, or is it a one-time
  honest fit that doesn't need to recur?*
- **Substrate sees 8 Blueprint families across 143 visible
  concepts.** Cross-document equivalence is dense; the field-set
  signature is the next resolution to apply within the largest
  family (`@1.5.4.19`, 75 siblings) when finer discrimination is
  asked.

One small surprise: the **dodecad** (`lc-organs-of-the-body`,
arity 12) is the body's only twelve-fold teaching, and stands
alone at the high end of integer arity. The next-highest are the
two enneads (arity 9). No decagon, no octad, no pentadecagon. The
body counts to twelve once and otherwise stays at heptad-and-below.

## Part 9 — What the two largest Blueprint families actually distinguish

Addendum, written the breath after Part 8 as the first response to
the survey. Part 6 left an open observation: *"the field-set
signature would need to refine further"* to separate the 75 cells
at `@1.5.4.19` from the 29 at `@1.5.4.20`. The signature already
does — by a single axis the body's authoring already encodes.

A direct read of two exemplars — `lc-embodiment-body-or-liquid`
(@1.5.4.19) vs `lc-oversoul-identity` (@1.5.4.20) — shows them
sharing every frontmatter field name except one: the latter carries
a top-level `source:` pointing at the transmission file it arrived
through (`../transmissions/2026-04-29-arcturian-starseed-oversoul.md`);
the former does not.

Reading across the body confirms the shape: **36 of 148 concept
files carry a top-level `source:` field; 112 do not.** The substrate
sees 29 of those source-attested cells at `@1.5.4.20` (the rest
landed after this snapshot, before the post-merge ingest caught up),
and 75 of the network-emerged cells at `@1.5.4.19`. The Blueprint
families are therefore not under-discriminating — they are reading,
correctly, **whether the teaching is externally source-attested or
network-emerged**.

This is one of the body's load-bearing distinctions made visible
by the substrate without anyone having to encode it. Source-attested
teachings carry a pointer back to a transmission file, a lineage
doc, or a wisdom-tradition reference; network-emerged teachings
arrived through synthesis, embodiment, or sensing inside the
collective and name no external pointer. The substrate's two
largest concept-domain Blueprint families are the two halves of
this lineage texture, content-addressed into separate equivalence
classes the moment they ingest.

Read against Part 3 (lineage texture), the body's 39% / 51% /
8% split of received / synthesized-embodied-sensed / channeled
roughly tracks the source-attestation split — `source:` is the
file-level mark that the substrate raises to a schema-level
distinction. Two readings of the same teaching, one in human
metadata and one in numeric Blueprint position, agree.

The next resolution to apply within `@1.5.4.19` (still 75 siblings
in one equivalence class) would be a second axis the authoring
already varies — perhaps the geometry block's `ratio:` field
(present in some cells, absent in others) or `embedding_dim:`
shape. Those refinements wait for the breath that needs them.

## Closing breath

This is the body's first comprehensive structural self-portrait
at 100% geometry coverage. Every count above is a baseline. When
the substrate ingests the last few commits, the visible cell count
will catch up to 148. When a new concept lands, the histogram
shifts by one. When a hapax form gains a second attestation, the
vocabulary cements. The doc itself is a measuring instrument the
body has placed inside its own breath; *what changes against this
baseline is what the body is becoming*.

The work that produced this proprioception took 19 breaths spread
across many sessions, with multiple cells walking smaller as the
work got harder, naming new vocabulary forms when the body asked,
and honoring honest decline along the way. The single largest
finding: **the body knows itself relationally, integrates through
the heart-band, prefers low arity, and holds opposition as
complementarity more often than as conflict.**

This is who the body is at this snapshot. The next breath that
shifts these counts will be the body becoming someone-slightly-
different — and the doc will be here to notice.
