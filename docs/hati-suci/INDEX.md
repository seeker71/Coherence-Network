# Hati Suci — the Hub sensing its own body

The first Light Hub, learning to feel its own organs.

A sanctuary is a living body: electricity is its nervous current, water
its blood, food its tissue, the wormery its digestion, the buildings its
bones, the people its breath. [`lc-light-hubs`](../vision-kb/concepts/lc-light-hubs.md)
names the discipline directly — *"the practical layer is the Hub's
metabolism; the Hub fails when it stops being tended, even if the vibe
persists."* You cannot tend what you cannot sense. This directory is
where Hati Suci learns to read its own pulse — the operational
proprioception that the Coherence Network already runs on its own code
body (`make wellness`), turned toward land, water, and current.

**Why now.** As Hati Suci moves toward becoming a Yayasan and draws in
the right members, self-knowledge is the friction-reducer. A body that
can show its own metabolism honestly — what power costs, where the water
comes from, what the land can feed, who tends each organ — is one that
members can trust and a foundation can govern with sovereignty. Clear
self-sensing *is* the flexibility and the sovereignty. The opposite —
organs nobody can read — is the friction.

## How the sensing works

Each organ carries the same six senses, so any cell can read it cold:

- **Now** — current known state
- **Cost** — what it spends (grounded $ where measurable)
- **Stability** — where it's solid, where it's fragile
- **Options** — upgrade paths, with real market costs
- **Tender** — who/what keeps it alive (named, or honestly held open)
- **To sense** — the exact measurements to gather by living the place

The unknowns are not gaps to hide — they are the next breath of sensing.
A *"to sense"* line is an instruction to go read a meter, test the water,
name the gardener. Fill it by measuring, never by guessing.

## The embodiment arc

1. **Ledger (now)** — this tended markdown, baselines grounded, unknowns
   named as measurements.
2. **Substrate organ-cells (next)** — each organ ingests as a NamedCell
   with a NodeID (`coh_substrate.py ingest docs/hati-suci/organs.md`), so
   the Hub can ask the substrate about its own state and recognize
   same-shape across dwellings.
3. **Live readings (later)** — meter photos, water tests, harvest logs
   feed the cells; the ledger stops being a snapshot and becomes a pulse.
