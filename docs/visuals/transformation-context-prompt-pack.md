# Transformation Context Prompt Pack

Use this pack when generating new vision images that need to show both:

- existing structures transformed with minimal physical energy
- brand-new embodiments once the pattern is coherent enough to build from first principles

## Shared visual principles

- no picket fences or decorative separation
- connected thresholds, shared commons, visible hospitality
- people actively using the space, not empty architectural rendering
- transformation is legible through use, warmth, and relationship
- city, urban, suburban, and rural contexts should all feel invitational

## City

### Transform what is here

`visuals:photorealistic existing apartment tower in a dense city retuned into a shared living field, visible rooftop garden and open commons floor and welcoming lobby tea station, residents moving between private rooms and shared nourishment areas, warm civic atmosphere, no luxury branding, no hard separation, highly believable present-day retrofit`

### Build what follows

`visuals:photorealistic future city housing designed from coherence, dense vertical village with civic hearths and shared kitchens and quiet recovery rooms and planted terraces, warm earthen and timber materials integrated into urban density, emotionally alive, believable near-future architecture`

## Urban

### Transform what is here

`visuals:photorealistic existing mixed-use neighborhood block transformed through low-energy changes, storefront turned provision house, visible kitchen and repair table and long shared table on the sidewalk edge, bikes and people moving slowly, open thresholds, no commercial extraction energy, realistic urban fabric`

### Build what follows

`visuals:photorealistic future urban commons block designed for shared nourishment and making and gathering, open courtyards and sheltered arcades and visible community kitchen, dense but breathable, rich textures, many people of different backgrounds, coherent and grounded`

## Suburban

### Transform what is here

`visuals:photorealistic North American suburban lane transformed into connected commons with no visible fences, front yards flowing into shared gardens and fruit trees and shaded seating and workshop garage, children moving freely, adults cooking and talking, low-energy changes using existing houses, believable present-day transition`

### Build what follows

`visuals:photorealistic suburban field reorganized into clustered village pattern, existing roads softened into walkable lanes, homes connected by commons gardens and shared bathhouse and long table and small gathering circles, no defensive boundaries, peaceful and immediately inhabitable`

## Rural

### Transform what is here

`visuals:photorealistic existing rural house and barn and greenhouse transformed into a shared anchor for a growing field, common kitchen and tool room and bathing area and guest welcome visible, pathways linking structures, food garden integrated, no homestead isolation energy, warm and practical`

### Build what follows

`visuals:photorealistic new rural living cluster grown from coherence, curved earthen and timber structures around shared fire and gardens and bathing and nourishment hall, connected paths, no fences, land-rooted and elegant, many generations present, believable sacred ordinariness`

## Notes for generation

- transformed scenes should feel close to present-day reality
- brand-new embodiment scenes can be more visionary, but still grounded
- avoid empty concept-art polish; favor inhabited warmth
- if a context starts feeling exclusive or niche, widen the cultural invitation
