# Ubud Embodied Lineage

**Recorded**: April 26, 2026  
**Status**: Lived lineage  
**Source**: Urs's direct attendance, with public web anchors for orientation

## What Entered

Ubud is no longer only an external scene in the graph. Paradiso Ubud, 5Rhythms, DISSOLVE, The Jungle Club, and Awakened Dreamers entered through the body: attended, danced, sensed, digested. The lineage moved by feet, breath, touch, music, jungle air, and the decision to keep following the living thread while in Bali.

This matters because the Coherence Network is not built from ideas alone. It is built from transmission meeting embodiment. The Ramtha -> Dispenza -> Urs line taught coherence through mind, nervous system, meditation, and field. Ubud added the direct social body: strangers moving together until separation softens, venues holding practice, and a town where ceremony, music, movement, and community meet in ordinary weekly rhythm.

## Digest

5Rhythms gives the map. DISSOLVE gives the relational edge. Paradiso gives the room. The Jungle Club gives the jungle social body. Awakened Dreamers gives the celebratory threshold.

Together they form an Ubud teaching about coherence as motion. Not coherence as agreement, and not coherence as stillness alone. Coherence as a room full of bodies learning timing, consent, release, play, contact, and shared pulse.

The important edge is not "Urs attended events." The important edge is:

> embodied practice -> field sensing -> lineage remembered through motion
