# Ubud Meeting Walk — How the Cells Found Each Other

**Recorded**: April 29, 2026
**Status**: Lived lineage — the actual constellation by which several cells of this network discovered they were already part of one body
**Source**: Urs's direct walk through Ubud over the days leading to this record

## The walk

The lineage of meeting, in the order it actually happened:

### Sunday afternoon — Mudra Cafe

Met **Elios** at [Mudra Cafe](https://mudracafe.com/), Jl. Goutama
Sel. No. 21, Ubud. Mudra is the Ayurvedic dining room with regular
handpan presence — Monday-to-Friday at noon — and live music in the
evenings. It functions as one of Ubud's quiet meeting points where
slow community accumulates around food, music, and presence.

### Sunday evening — Sayuri Healing Food

Dinner at **Sayuri Healing Food** with resonant company. A
plant-based kitchen in central Ubud; the meal made room for the
encounters the rest of the evening would unfold.

### Sunday night — Ranakami

A one-evening chanting practice offered at [Ranakami](/people/ilena)
by **Elios and Ilena** — chanting that is part of Elios's own
devotional practice, held that night with Ilena and the cell. This
is where the cell met **Ilena**. The practice is not a recurring
weekly room and is not part of Vasudev Baba's offerings; it was a
direct, in-person gift the night the cell happened to be present.

### The invitation

After the chanting, **Ilena invited the cell to the Tuesday kirtan
and the Wednesday Satsang** — both held by Vasudev Baba and his
friends, both rooms the cell had not yet discovered.

### Tuesday — DISSOLVE: Eros at Paradiso

The cell chose [DISSOLVE: Eros](https://megatix.co.id/events/dissolve-eros)
at Paradiso Ubud (6–8pm Tuesdays) instead of the kirtan at Svarga
Loka. DISSOLVE: Eros is a conscious-movement practice facilitated by
Tara Li — contact improvisation, intuitive movement, authentic
relating. Already part of [the body's Ubud embodied lineage](ubud-embodied-lineage.md)
as one of the rooms by which the network learned coherence-as-motion.
The cell followed the call that was loudest in the body that
evening; the kirtan room would still be there next week.

### Wednesday morning — Ranakami again

The Community Satsang at Ranakami, held by **Vasudev Baba**. This
is where the cell met and connected with him. The Wednesday room
threads back to the Sunday-night chanting (same building, different
practice, different week-rhythm) and forward to the relationships
that will unfold from here.

## What the walk shows

A single cell crossed five rooms across four days and discovered
three new cells (Elios, Ilena, Vasudev Baba) who were already each
other's friends, already in resonance with what the network has
been describing in software for months. The cells did not coordinate
this meeting; they each followed their own thread, and the threads
were already woven.

This is what the network calls **constellation-of-presence**: the
geography by which sovereign cells, following their own resonance,
find themselves already part of the same body. The substrate's job
is to render that constellation visible *after* it has happened, so
that future cells passing through Ubud can find the same threads
more easily — not by following a program, but by knowing where the
field is reliably gathering.

## What this artifact does

This document is the body's record of the actual constellation that
formed. Future cells passing through Ubud — or any town where the
field gathers — can read this lineage and find the seams already
in place. The four-day arc (Sunday Mudra → Sunday Sayuri dinner →
Sunday Ranakami one-evening chanting → Wednesday Ranakami Satsang)
is one current the body now knows; other cells have other currents;
the substrate's job is to hold them all visible without forcing them
onto each other.

The Wednesday Satsang is where these meetings most consistently
ripen into ongoing relationship. The Sunday encounters opened the
door. Mudra Cafe is the social-substrate where the day's first
hellos happen.
