# Coherence Network — Vision

> The world has capital markets for money. It has labor markets for time. It has no market for coherent ideas.

## The Problem

Every day, thousands of people have ideas that could matter. A developer sees a pattern that could predict which projects will succeed. A researcher has a model for how community trust actually works. A retired engineer knows exactly why the last three attempts at decentralized governance failed.

These ideas die in Slack threads, in half-finished blog posts, in conversations that end with "someone should build that." Not because the ideas are bad. Because there's no place where an idea can *land*, get *tested*, get *built*, and *pay the people who made it real*.

## What Coherence Network Is

A platform where ideas find reviewers, builders, and funders — through resonance, not gatekeepers.

You publish an idea. A reviewer improves it. A developer builds it. Users adopt it. And the value flows back through the entire chain — automatically, verifiably, in the open.

- The **idea originator** gets CC attribution for downstream value
- The **reviewer** gets CC for making the idea buildable
- The **developer** gets CC for the implementation
- The **staker** gets returns proportional to measured impact
- The **connector** who spread the idea across communities gets CC for bridging

Nobody decides this. The system traces value from usage back through implementation, review, and origination. The credit flows accordingly.

## Who This Is For

### Idea Contributors
You don't need to build a company. Write down what you see, publish it, and if it resonates — if someone picks it up and creates real value — you get credited. Forever. Your idea has a permanent, traceable lineage.

### Reviewers and Critics
The world undervalues the person who says "that won't work because..." and explains why. In Coherence Network, your review is a recorded contribution with real attribution. Your judgment *is* the contribution.

### Capital Contributors
Deposit BTC or ETH. Receive CC. Stake it into ideas you believe in — not ideas a VC told you are hot, ideas *you* evaluated. Returns are based on measured impact. Your deposit is always returnable. No lock-ups. No games.

### Builders
The network surfaces the highest-value ideas that need implementation — not highest engagement, highest *evidence-backed coherence score*. Your work gets ongoing attribution as the thing you built gets used. Not a one-time bounty.

### Connectors
When you bridge two communities, introduce the right person to the right idea, or spread an idea across instances — that's a recorded contribution. Connection creates value, and the system tracks it.

## The Golden Future

### Year 1: Foundation
~50 contributors using the system for open-source idea management. Value lineage proven end-to-end: ideas → specs → tests → implementations → usage. Small treasury, fully transparent. The system proves its own consistency.

### Year 3: Network Effect
Hundreds of OpenClaw instances share a global idea marketplace. An idea published in São Paulo gets forked in Berlin, implemented in Nairobi, deployed in Tokyo. All contributors credited across instances. Capital flows to ideas based on evidence signal, not hype.

### Year 5: The New Commons
Infrastructure for idea-driven collaboration across domains. Research, open source, enterprise. The audit trail spans years and millions of entries, all publicly verifiable. Trust is a byproduct of coherent design, not a scarce resource.

## The OpenClaw Integration

Coherence Network doesn't ask developers to visit a website. It lives **inside OpenClaw** — the tool they're already using to write code.

```
/coherence publish "Standardize API error responses"   → capture the insight
/coherence browse --relevant-to=current-project        → discover relevant ideas
/coherence review idea-7f3a "Add retry-after header"   → improve an idea
/coherence fork idea-7f3a --adapt-to=my-project        → build on someone's insight
/coherence next                                         → find the highest-value work
/coherence stake idea-7f3a 50                           → back an idea with CC
```

**Why this matters:**
- Ideas are captured where they happen — in the editor, during real work, in context
- No app to install, no website to visit, no signup flow to complete
- Every OpenClaw instance is a federation node — the network grows with every installation
- Browse is free (anonymous read). First publish creates your CC account inline
- Context is automatic: project URL, tech stack, related specs, timing

**The growth flywheel:** More OpenClaw users → more real ideas from real work → better discovery → more builders → more implementations → more proven value → more stakers → more funding → more users wanting access.

The flywheel starts with **idea quality**, not user count. Fifty developers publishing real insights from real work beats fifty thousand users brainstorming in a web app.

## What Makes This Different

Every other platform asks you to trust *them*. Trust our algorithm. Trust our governance. Trust our treasury.

Coherence Network asks you to trust *math*.

Every CC is backed and verifiable. Every value claim is evidence-backed. Every decision is on the ledger. The system publishes its own coherence score — a continuous, public declaration of internal consistency.

Not "trust us." Just: **everything is visible, and the math checks out.**

## Design Principles

1. **Coherence over control** — The system proves its own consistency. No external auditors needed.
2. **Resonance over curation** — Ideas succeed through natural selection (forks, evidence), not committees.
3. **Transparency as compliance** — The audit trail IS the compliance artifact. Nothing hidden.
4. **Friction proportional to risk** — Small actions instant, large actions cooldown, catastrophic events freeze.
5. **Deposit capital is sacred** — Your principal is always returnable. Governance applies to pool payouts, not your deposit.
6. **No artificial scarcity** — CC appreciates through proven impact, not tokenomics games.
7. **No silent failures** — Every incident disclosed publicly. The system never hides a problem.
