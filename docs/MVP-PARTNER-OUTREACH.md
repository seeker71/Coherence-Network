# MVP Partner Outreach Starter Pack

Last updated: 2026-03-11
Owner: `seeker71`

## Goal

Secure early conversations with high-fit projects and funders to validate demand and shape pilot onboarding.

## Target Profiles

### Project-side targets
- Team has a real near-term funding or support need.
- Team can commit to light reporting and quality standards.
- Mission fit with ecosystem outcomes you want to accelerate.

### Funder/investor-side targets
- Interested in measurable impact and transparent project progress.
- Comfortable with pilot-stage structure and staged commitments.
- Willing to participate in a lightweight feedback loop.

## 10-Target Sprint Plan

Week 1 outreach minimum:
- 6 project-side contacts.
- 4 funder/investor-side contacts.
- Log every message, response, and next step.

## First-Touch Email Template

Subject: Pilot collaboration for trusted project-funding marketplace

Hi {Name},

I’m building a pilot marketplace to connect high-quality projects with aligned funders, with transparent progress and trust checks from day one.

I thought of {Org} because {specific relevance}.

Would you be open to a short 20-minute call next week to explore pilot fit?

If useful, I can send a one-page overview in advance.

Best,  
{Your name}

## Discovery Call Script (20 minutes)

1. Context (3 min)
- Why this exists, who it is for, what “pilot” means.

2. Their goals and constraints (7 min)
- What outcomes matter most in the next 90 days?
- What would make this collaboration useful vs noisy?

3. Pilot fit test (7 min)
- Required trust/process expectations.
- Data and reporting cadence.
- Initial commitment shape.

4. Close with concrete next step (3 min)
- Share one-pager, schedule follow-up, or mark as no-fit.

## Follow-up Rules

- If no reply in 3 business days, send follow-up #1.
- If no reply in 7 business days, send follow-up #2 and close loop.
- Archive low-fit contacts quickly to protect cycle time.

## Weekly Deliverables

- Updated target table (10+ contacts).
- Response summary (reply rate, call conversion rate).
- Top 3 blockers + mitigation actions for next week.
